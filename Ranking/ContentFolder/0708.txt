<p><b>Kava Kava</b> is a British <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a> band/live dance act from <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, England featuring the vocals, production and songwriting of Pat Fulgoni. The current line-up features Pat Fulgoni on vocals, electronica, strings and beats. Mitch Cockman on bass guitar, Alex Krzesinski on guitar, Ben Wallbanks on drums, Ian Bailey and Rob Crisp on saxophones  <sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>They started as a four-piece live <a href="/wiki/Psychedelic_music" title="Psychedelic music">psychedelic</a> <a href="/wiki/Funk" title="Funk">funk</a> outfit with heavy <a href="/wiki/George_Clinton_(funk_musician)" class="mw-redirect" title="George Clinton (funk musician)">George Clinton</a> influences with Pat Fulgoni on vocals, keyboards and brass, Jason Riley on bass, Mathew Bond on guitar, Chris Sykes on drums. In the UK Kava Kava cut their teeth through the underground festival/party scene signing to Delerium Records touring Europe and the UK with amongst others label mates <a href="/wiki/Porcupine_Tree" title="Porcupine Tree">Porcupine Tree</a>, the <a href="/wiki/NME" title="NME">NME</a> describing the band as "gorgeous new psychedelic funk" for their debut 'You Can Live Here'
</p><p>The band expanded into a six piece and released 2004's 'Maui'<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> album on independent label <a href="/w/index.php?title=Chocolate_Fireguard_Records&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Chocolate Fireguard Records (page does not exist)">Chocolate Fireguard Records</a>. The music on 'Maui' is more dance, electronic, funk and <a href="/wiki/Disco" title="Disco">disco</a> influenced. They have notched up tours of the United States, <a href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" class="mw-redirect" title="People&#39;s Republic of China">China</a> and all over <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a> including <a href="/wiki/Glastonbury_Festival" title="Glastonbury Festival">Glastonbury Festival</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sziget_Festival" title="Sziget Festival">Sziget Festival</a>, <a href="/wiki/SXSW" class="mw-redirect" title="SXSW">SXSW</a> Festival, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Marktrock_Festival&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Marktrock Festival (page does not exist)">Marktrock Festival</a>, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Trutnov_Festival&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Trutnov Festival (page does not exist)">Trutnov Festival</a>, <a href="/wiki/Midi_Modern_Music_Festival" class="mw-redirect" title="Midi Modern Music Festival">Midi Modern Music Festival</a>, Beijing and their music has been featured in a number of films and TV shows including <a href="/wiki/Dirt_(TV_series)" title="Dirt (TV series)">Dirt (TV series)</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Boys_%26_Girls_Guide_To_Getting_Down" class="mw-redirect" title="Boys &amp; Girls Guide To Getting Down">Boys &amp; Girls Guide To Getting Down</a>, <a href="/wiki/John_From_Cincinnati" class="mw-redirect" title="John From Cincinnati">John From Cincinnati</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hollywood_Sex_Wars" title="Hollywood Sex Wars">Hollywood Sex Wars</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/w/index.php?title=LA_Riding_Club&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="LA Riding Club (page does not exist)">LA Riding Club</a> and <a href="/wiki/Golden_Globe" class="mw-redirect" title="Golden Globe">Golden Globe</a> award–winning TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Weeds_(TV_series)" title="Weeds (TV series)">Weeds</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and played by tastemaker DJs such as <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sat_Bisla&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Sat Bisla (page does not exist)">Sat Bisla</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nic_Harcourt" title="Nic Harcourt">Nic Harcourt</a> from <a href="/wiki/KCRW" title="KCRW">KCRW</a>. Pat Fulgoni continues to write music and tour as Kava Kava whilst collaborating with numerous other artists including many dance music artists signed to <a href="/wiki/Hospital_Records" title="Hospital Records">Hospital Records</a>
</p>
