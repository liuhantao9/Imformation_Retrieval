<p><b>Isabella Amaryllis Charlotte Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe</b> (born 3 March 1980) is an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Socialite" title="Socialite">socialite</a>, <a href="/wiki/Actress" class="mw-redirect" title="Actress">actress</a> and <a href="/wiki/Model_(person)" title="Model (person)">model</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isabella_Calthorpe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Calthorpe was born on 3 March 1980 in <a href="/wiki/Royal_Hampshire_County_Hospital" title="Royal Hampshire County Hospital">Royal Hampshire County Hospital</a> in <a href="/wiki/Winchester" title="Winchester">Winchester</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She is the daughter of <a href="/wiki/John_Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe" title="John Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe">John Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe</a> and Lady Mary-Gaye Georgiana Lorna Curzon. Her paternal grandfather is <a href="/wiki/Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe_baronets" title="Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe baronets">Sir Richard Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe, 2nd Baronet</a> and her maternal grandfather is <a href="/wiki/Edward_Curzon,_6th_Earl_Howe" title="Edward Curzon, 6th Earl Howe">Edward Curzon, 6th Earl Howe</a>. She was named Isabella Amaryllis Charlotte and came to be known by intimates as Bellie.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>She has two full siblings (elder sister Georgiana, an artist, and younger brother Jacobi), and four half-siblings: two from her father's second marriage, including actress <a href="/wiki/Gabriella_Wilde" title="Gabriella Wilde">Gabriella Calthorpe</a>; one from her mother's first marriage; and one from her mother's third marriage, the actress <a href="/wiki/Cressida_Bonas" title="Cressida Bonas">Cressida Bonas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Calthorpe was educated at <a href="/wiki/Heathfield_School,_Ascot" title="Heathfield School, Ascot">Heathfield School, Ascot</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Bradfield_College" title="Bradfield College">Bradfield College</a>, before going on to study <a href="/wiki/Classics" title="Classics">Classics</a> and <a href="/wiki/Art_history" title="Art history">Art History</a> at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Edinburgh" title="University of Edinburgh">University of Edinburgh</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isabella_Calthorpe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After attending the <a href="/wiki/London_Academy_of_Music_and_Dramatic_Art" title="London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art">London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art</a> from 2003 to 2005,<sup id="cite_ref-Isabella_Calthorpe_-_Biography_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Isabella_Calthorpe_-_Biography-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> she became a stage actress. She has also appeared in films <i><a href="/wiki/Stage_Beauty" title="Stage Beauty">Stage Beauty</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/How_to_Lose_Friends_%26_Alienate_People_(film)" title="How to Lose Friends &amp; Alienate People (film)">How to Lose Friends &amp; Alienate People</a></i>, and the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Harley_Street_(TV_series)" title="Harley Street (TV series)">Harley Street</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Trinity_(UK_TV_series)" class="mw-redirect" title="Trinity (UK TV series)">Trinity</a></i>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isabella_Calthorpe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Calthorpe is a friend of <a href="/wiki/Prince_William,_Duke_of_Cambridge" title="Prince William, Duke of Cambridge">Prince William, Duke of Cambridge</a>, and was a guest at <a href="/wiki/Wedding_of_Prince_William,_Duke_of_Cambridge,_and_Catherine_Middleton" class="mw-redirect" title="Wedding of Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, and Catherine Middleton">his wedding</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> While single, she appeared regularly in the <a href="/wiki/Society_reporting" title="Society reporting">society pages</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Tatler" title="Tatler">Tatler</a>,</i> <i><a href="/wiki/Harper%27s_Bazaar" title="Harper&#39;s Bazaar">Harper's Bazaar</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Hello!_(magazine)" title="Hello! (magazine)">Hello!</a></i> <sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 6 March 2013, she married Sam Branson, filmmaker, former model, and son of business tycoon <a href="/wiki/Richard_Branson" title="Richard Branson">Sir Richard Branson</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-timeswedding_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-timeswedding-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> The couple married at his father's private safari lodge near <a href="/wiki/Kruger_National_Park" title="Kruger National Park">Kruger National Park</a>, with guests including <a href="/wiki/Princess_Beatrice_of_York" title="Princess Beatrice of York">Princess Beatrice of York</a> and <a href="/wiki/Princess_Eugenie_of_York" title="Princess Eugenie of York">Princess Eugenie of York</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-timeswedding_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-timeswedding-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 9 September 2014, it was announced on the Virgin website that the couple were expecting their first child, and daughter Eva-Deia Branson was born on 19 February 2015.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> They had their second child, a son named Bluey Rafe Richard Branson, on 18 January 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ancestry">Ancestry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isabella_Calthorpe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Ancestry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She is a 9th great-granddaughter of <a href="/wiki/Charles_II_of_England_and_Scotland" class="mw-redirect" title="Charles II of England and Scotland">Charles II of England and Scotland</a> on both her mother's and father's side.  Her parents are third cousins, sharing Francis Horatio Fitzroy as a great-great-grandfather.
