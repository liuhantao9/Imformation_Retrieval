<p>The <b>HD postcode area</b>, also known as the <b>Huddersfield postcode area</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> is a group of postcode districts around <a href="/wiki/Brighouse" title="Brighouse">Brighouse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Holmfirth" title="Holmfirth">Holmfirth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Calderdale" title="Calderdale">Calderdale</a> <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_borough" title="Metropolitan borough">boroughs</a> of <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.
</p>
