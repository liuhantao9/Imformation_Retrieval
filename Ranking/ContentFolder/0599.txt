<p>The <b>A640</b> is a road in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> which runs between <a href="/wiki/Rochdale" title="Rochdale">Rochdale</a> in <a href="/wiki/Greater_Manchester" title="Greater Manchester">Greater Manchester</a> and <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> in <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Rochdale" title="Rochdale">Rochdale</a> terminus is the junction of Drake Street and Manchester Road (<a href="/wiki/A58_road" title="A58 road">A58</a>). From here, the road runs eastward through <a href="/w/index.php?title=Newbold,_Rochdale&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Newbold, Rochdale (page does not exist)">Newbold</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belfield,_Greater_Manchester" title="Belfield, Greater Manchester">Belfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/Firgrove" title="Firgrove">Firgrove</a>, <a href="/wiki/Milnrow" title="Milnrow">Milnrow</a>, <a href="/wiki/Newhey" title="Newhey">Newhey</a>, Ogden, <a href="/wiki/Denshaw" title="Denshaw">Denshaw</a>, Buckstones, Deanhead, <a href="/wiki/Scammonden" title="Scammonden">Scammonden</a>, <a href="/wiki/Outlane" title="Outlane">Outlane</a> and in <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> (<a href="/wiki/Salendine_Nook" title="Salendine Nook">Salendine Nook</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oakes,_Huddersfield" title="Oakes, Huddersfield">Oakes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marsh,_West_Yorkshire" class="mw-redirect" title="Marsh, West Yorkshire">Marsh</a>, before meeting the <a href="/wiki/A62_road" title="A62 road">A62</a> at Huddersfield's town centre ring road.)
</p><p>The road is often closed during periods of snow due to its remote nature. This cuts off the non-motorway route from <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> to <a href="/wiki/Manchester" title="Manchester">Manchester</a>.
</p>
