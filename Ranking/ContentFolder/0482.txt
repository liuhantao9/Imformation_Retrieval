	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Pennine Radio Limited</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>

		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><span style="font-size: small;"><span id="coordinates"><a href="/wiki/Geographic_coordinate_system" title="Geographic coordinate system">Coordinates</a>: <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Pennine_Radio_Limited&amp;params=53_39.01_N_1_47.111_W_region:GB_type:landmark"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">53°39.01′N</span> <span class="longitude">1°47.111′W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">&#xfeff; / &#xfeff;</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">53.65017°N 1.785183°W</span><span style="display:none">&#xfeff; / <span class="geo">53.65017; -1.785183</span></span></span></a></span></span></span>
</p><p><b>Pennine Radio Limited</b> is a <a href="/wiki/UK" class="mw-redirect" title="UK">UK</a> manufacturer of <a href="/wiki/Electronic_equipment" class="mw-redirect" title="Electronic equipment">electronic equipment</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transformer" title="Transformer">transformers</a> and <a href="/wiki/Inductors" class="mw-redirect" title="Inductors">inductors</a>, <a href="/wiki/Golf_cart" title="Golf cart">ride on electric golf carts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sheet_metal" title="Sheet metal">sheet metalwork</a> and <a href="/wiki/Computer" title="Computer">computer equipment</a>.<br />
Founded in 1958<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> the company started off producing <a href="/wiki/Receiver_(radio)" class="mw-redirect" title="Receiver (radio)">radio receivers</a> and adapters to enable the new <a href="/wiki/Band_III" title="Band III">band III</a> <a href="/wiki/ITV_Network" class="mw-redirect" title="ITV Network">ITV</a> <a href="/wiki/Television_broadcasts" class="mw-redirect" title="Television broadcasts">television broadcasts</a>. However they soon expanded into industrial electronics. In the early days they produced many innovative designs for the <a href="/wiki/Woolen" title="Woolen">woollen industry</a> which surrounded <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Pennine_Radio_Building.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/2/2e/Pennine_Radio_Building.jpg/220px-Pennine_Radio_Building.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/2/2e/Pennine_Radio_Building.jpg/330px-Pennine_Radio_Building.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/2/2e/Pennine_Radio_Building.jpg/440px-Pennine_Radio_Building.jpg 2x" data-file-width="753" data-file-height="566" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Pennine_Radio_Building.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>PRL headquarters, in <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a></div></div></div>
<p>Pennine Radio's Headquarters are in a Victorian grade 2 listed building on Fitzwilliam Street Huddersfield. 
</p><p>The company currently manufactures <a href="/wiki/Golf_cart" title="Golf cart">golf carts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transformer" title="Transformer">transformers</a>, and offers contract printed circuit board, electronics assembly, and sheet-metal fabrication. The company is wholly owned by one family. <sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
