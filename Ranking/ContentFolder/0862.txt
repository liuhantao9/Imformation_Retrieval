<p><b>John Whitaker</b> <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_British_Empire" title="Order of the British Empire">MBE</a> (born 5 August 1955, <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-bsj_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bsj-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> is a <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Equestrianism" title="Equestrianism">equestrian</a> and  <a href="/wiki/Olympic_Games" title="Olympic Games">Olympian</a> who competes in <a href="/wiki/Show_jumping" title="Show jumping">show jumping</a>. He has won numerous international medals including an olympic silver medal at the <a href="/wiki/1984_Summer_Olympics" title="1984 Summer Olympics">1984 Summer Olympics</a> held in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>. 
</p><p>He is also the managing director of John Whitaker International Limited which produces <a href="/wiki/Horse_tack" title="Horse tack">tack</a> and <a href="/wiki/List_of_equestrian_sports" title="List of equestrian sports">equestrian</a> clothing<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Showjumping">Showjumping</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Whitaker_(equestrian)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Showjumping">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Whitaker has enjoyed success across four decades. He has competed at World and European Championships and five olympic teams between 1984 and 2016<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>. 
</p><p>He has won the <a href="/wiki/Hickstead_Derby" class="mw-redirect" title="Hickstead Derby">Hickstead Derby</a> four times<sup id="cite_ref-:0_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Horses">Horses</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Whitaker_(equestrian)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Horses">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He is best known for his partnership with the horse <a href="/wiki/Milton_(horse)" title="Milton (horse)">Milton</a> with whom he won the Du Maunier Grand Prix at <a href="/wiki/Spruce_Meadows" title="Spruce Meadows">Spruce Meadows</a> and won over £1 million in prize money before being retired in 1994<sup id="cite_ref-:0_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>. 
</p><p>However he won his olympic medal in 1984 on Ryan's Son who he was given at the age of 18 and had a partnership with for 14 years<sup id="cite_ref-:0_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=John_Whitaker_(equestrian)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Whitaker family is well known for its showjumping prowess<sup id="cite_ref-:0_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>. John is married to Clare Barr and has three children – Joanne, Louise and Robert. <a href="/wiki/Robert_Whitaker_(equestrian)" title="Robert Whitaker (equestrian)">Robert Whitaker</a> has successfully show jumped at the highest levels. John's niece <a href="/wiki/Ellen_Whitaker" title="Ellen Whitaker">Ellen Whitaker</a>, nephew <a href="/wiki/William_Whitaker_(equestrian)" title="William Whitaker (equestrian)">William Whitaker</a> and younger brother <a href="/wiki/Michael_Whitaker" title="Michael Whitaker">Michael Whitaker</a> are also show jumpers. 
</p><p>John began riding at the age of 6, taught by his mother who ran a riding school. As children, John and his brother <a href="/wiki/Michael_Whitaker" title="Michael Whitaker">Michael</a> delivered milk using a horse and cart until they were 16 and began to show jump professionally<sup id="cite_ref-:0_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>.
</p>
