<p><b>Hudderfield Grammar School</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Coeducational" class="mw-redirect" title="Coeducational">coeducational</a> <a href="/wiki/Independent_school" title="Independent school">independent school</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> in <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England. The school was established in 1995 on the site of the former independent St David's School, which closed on 1 September 1995.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  St David's school had been purchased and merged with the former independent Kayes College in 1995 <sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Since 2007 the school has been owned and operated by the <a href="/wiki/Cognita" title="Cognita">Cognita</a> Group. It is the largest independent school in Huddersfield to offer both primary and secondary education.
</p><p>In 2014, Cognita invested £3.5&#160;million at Huddersfield Grammar School to further enhance the school's site and facilities.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
