<p><b>Outlane</b> is a village near <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England with a population of 710 according to the 2001 census. <a href="/wiki/Historic_counties_of_England" title="Historic counties of England">Historically</a> part of the <a href="/wiki/West_Riding_of_Yorkshire" title="West Riding of Yorkshire">West Riding of Yorkshire</a>, it is situated 3.7 miles (6&#160;km) north-west of Huddersfield town centre
</p><p>The village is situated next to the <a href="/wiki/M62_motorway" title="M62 motorway">M62 motorway</a> near junction 23 close to the <a href="/wiki/Calderdale" title="Calderdale">Calderdale</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a> district boundary. The <a href="/wiki/A640_road" title="A640 road">A640</a> New Hey Road passes through the village.
</p><p>Outlane Cricket Club objected to the building of the motorway in the 1960s as it would go through their ground. However the <a href="/wiki/Department_for_Transport" title="Department for Transport">Ministry of Transport</a> turned it down. Outlane has a golf course that borders the motorway.
</p><p>Slack Lane is the location of <a href="/wiki/Slack_Roman_fort" class="mw-redirect" title="Slack Roman fort">Slack Roman fort</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> whose Roman name was possibly Cambodunum.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
