<p><b>Golcar United Football Club</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Golcar" title="Golcar">Golcar</a>, <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England. They are currently members of the <a href="/wiki/North_West_Counties_Football_League" title="North West Counties Football League">North West Counties League</a> Division One North and play at Longfield Avenue.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Golcar_United_F.C.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Golcar joined the <a href="/wiki/West_Riding_County_Amateur_Football_League" title="West Riding County Amateur Football League">West Riding County Amateur League</a> in 1934 and were runners-up in their first season in the league.<sup id="cite_ref-NLM1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NLM1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> After finishing bottom of the table in 1937–38, the club left the league.<sup id="cite_ref-NLM1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NLM1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, they later rejoined.
</p><p>Golcar won the West Riding County Challenge Cup in 2000–01,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and were Premier Division runners-up in 2003–04. The following season saw them win the league title.<sup id="cite_ref-NLM2_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NLM2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> They were runners-up in 2014–15, and champions in 2017–18.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> They retained the league title the following season, and were promoted to Division One North of the <a href="/wiki/North_West_Counties_Football_League" title="North West Counties Football League">North West Counties League</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
