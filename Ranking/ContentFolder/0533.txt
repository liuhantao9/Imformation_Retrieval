	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Marsh, Huddersfield</h1>

<p><b>Marsh</b> is a suburb of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> that is situated 0.93 miles (1.5&#160;km) west of the town centre.<sup id="cite_ref-:map:_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:map:-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the 2001 census the population was recorded as 4,482. It is said that the area is so-called because it was wetland before it was reclaimed.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It used to be known as Paddock-with-Marsh. The <a href="/wiki/A640_road" title="A640 road">A640 road</a> between Huddersfield town centre and Junction 23 of the <a href="/wiki/M62_motorway" title="M62 motorway">M62 motorway</a> runs through Marsh.<sup id="cite_ref-:map:_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:map:-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> This thoroughfare is known as Westbourne Road and is Marsh's main shopping area. One of Huddersfield's private schools, Huddersfield Grammar, is situated on Luck Lane.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_CCC" class="mw-redirect" title="Yorkshire CCC">Yorkshire</a> and <a href="/wiki/England_national_cricket_team" class="mw-redirect" title="England national cricket team">England</a> <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricketer</a> <a href="/wiki/Wilfred_Rhodes" title="Wilfred Rhodes">Wilfred Rhodes</a> and his family lived in Marsh for over 40 years.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The actor <a href="/wiki/James_Mason" title="James Mason">James Mason</a> originated from this area before going to <a href="/wiki/Cambridge_University" class="mw-redirect" title="Cambridge University">Cambridge University</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hollywood" title="Hollywood">Hollywood</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Holy_Trinity_Church,_Huddersfield" title="Holy Trinity Church, Huddersfield">Holy Trinity Church</a> lies in the eastern part of the suburb, just outside Huddersfield town centre, and was built between 1816 and 1819.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
