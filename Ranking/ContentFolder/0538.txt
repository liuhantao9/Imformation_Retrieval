<p><b>Armitage Bridge</b> is a village approximately 3 miles (4.8&#160;km) south of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Holme_Valley" title="Holme Valley">Holme Valley</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England. It is situated between <a href="/wiki/Berry_Brow" title="Berry Brow">Berry Brow</a> and <a href="/wiki/South_Crosland" title="South Crosland">South Crosland</a> and straddles the <a href="/wiki/River_Holme" title="River Holme">River Holme</a>. The village has a public house, a cricket club and a renowned ukulele shop.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Brooke_Mill">Brooke Mill</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Armitage_Bridge&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Brooke Mill">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Armitage_Bridge_Mills_-_geograph.org.uk_-_489370.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/Armitage_Bridge_Mills_-_geograph.org.uk_-_489370.jpg/220px-Armitage_Bridge_Mills_-_geograph.org.uk_-_489370.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="137" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/Armitage_Bridge_Mills_-_geograph.org.uk_-_489370.jpg/330px-Armitage_Bridge_Mills_-_geograph.org.uk_-_489370.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/Armitage_Bridge_Mills_-_geograph.org.uk_-_489370.jpg/440px-Armitage_Bridge_Mills_-_geograph.org.uk_-_489370.jpg 2x" data-file-width="640" data-file-height="399" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Armitage_Bridge_Mills_-_geograph.org.uk_-_489370.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Brooke Mill</div></div></div>
<p>The village is dominated by the large former <a href="/wiki/Woolen" title="Woolen">woollen</a> mill of John Brooke and Sons, reputedly the oldest family business in the country, having been founded in 1541.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The mill has now been converted into the Yorkshire Technology and Office Park.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="St_Pauls">St Pauls</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Armitage_Bridge&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: St Pauls">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:St_Paul,_Armitage_Bridge_1_(3301402270).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/97/St_Paul%2C_Armitage_Bridge_1_%283301402270%29.jpg/220px-St_Paul%2C_Armitage_Bridge_1_%283301402270%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="174" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/97/St_Paul%2C_Armitage_Bridge_1_%283301402270%29.jpg/330px-St_Paul%2C_Armitage_Bridge_1_%283301402270%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/97/St_Paul%2C_Armitage_Bridge_1_%283301402270%29.jpg/440px-St_Paul%2C_Armitage_Bridge_1_%283301402270%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2293" data-file-height="1814" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:St_Paul,_Armitage_Bridge_1_(3301402270).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>St Paul's Church</div></div></div>
<p>The local church is St Paul's and it was built in 1848, with a donation from the Brooke family. Partially destroyed in 1987, by fire, and rebuilt the same year.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The church's current clergy is the Rev Stephen Gott.
</p>
