<p>The <b>Calderdale Royal Hospital</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Hospital" title="Hospital">hospital</a> situated in the <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> town of <a href="/wiki/Halifax,_West_Yorkshire" title="Halifax, West Yorkshire">Halifax</a>. It is located in the suburb of <a href="/wiki/Salterhebble" title="Salterhebble">Salterhebble</a> and provides general services, emergency services and some specialist services. It is managed by <a href="/wiki/Calderdale_and_Huddersfield_NHS_Foundation_Trust" title="Calderdale and Huddersfield NHS Foundation Trust">Calderdale and Huddersfield NHS Foundation Trust</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Calderdale_Royal_Hospital&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The hospital, which replaced the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Halifax_Infirmary" title="Royal Halifax Infirmary">Royal Halifax Infirmary</a>, was procured under a <a href="/wiki/Private_Finance_Initiative" class="mw-redirect" title="Private Finance Initiative">Private Finance Initiative</a> contract in 1998. The new hospital was built by <a href="/wiki/Lend_Lease_Project_Management_%26_Construction" title="Lend Lease Project Management &amp; Construction">Bovis Construction</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The cost of building the hospital, originally budgeted at £34.8 million in 1994, was £103 million by 2001.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It started providing services to patients in April 2001 and was officially opened by <a href="/wiki/Anne,_Princess_Royal" title="Anne, Princess Royal">HRH Princess Anne</a> on 15 March 2004.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Facilities management services are provided by <a href="/wiki/ISS_A/S" title="ISS A/S">ISS</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Proposals to close the Accident and Emergency Department in February 2016 provoked a demonstration of 5,000 people in Huddersfield and a petition signed by 60,000 people.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
