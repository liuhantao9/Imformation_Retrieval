<p><b>Hall Bower</b> is a small <a href="/wiki/Hamlet_(place)" title="Hamlet (place)">hamlet</a> lying 2 miles (3.5&#160;km) south of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England.<sup id="cite_ref-OS110_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OS110-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It sits in the shadow of <a href="/wiki/Castle_Hill,_Huddersfield" title="Castle Hill, Huddersfield">Castle Hill</a>, just above the village of <a href="/wiki/Newsome" title="Newsome">Newsome</a>. Fields in the village next to Hall Bower CC were recently converted into rugby league pitches for use by <a href="/wiki/Newsome_Panthers" title="Newsome Panthers"> Newsome Panthers ARLFC</a>.
</p>
