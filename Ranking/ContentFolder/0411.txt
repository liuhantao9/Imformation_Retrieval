<p><b>Yorkshire and the Humber</b> is a <a href="/wiki/European_Parliament_constituency" title="European Parliament constituency">constituency</a> of the <a href="/wiki/European_Parliament" title="European Parliament">European Parliament</a>. It elects six <a href="/wiki/Members_of_the_European_Parliament" class="mw-redirect" title="Members of the European Parliament">Members of the European Parliament</a> (MEPs) using the <a href="/wiki/D%27Hondt_method" title="D&#39;Hondt method">D'Hondt method</a> of <a href="/wiki/Party-list_proportional_representation" title="Party-list proportional representation">party-list proportional representation</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Boundaries">Boundaries</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yorkshire_and_the_Humber_(European_Parliament_constituency)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Boundaries">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The constituency corresponds to the <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_and_the_Humber" title="Yorkshire and the Humber">Yorkshire and the Humber</a> region of the United Kingdom, comprising the ceremonial counties of <a href="/wiki/South_Yorkshire" title="South Yorkshire">South Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/East_Riding_of_Yorkshire" title="East Riding of Yorkshire">East Riding of Yorkshire</a> and parts of <a href="/wiki/North_Yorkshire" title="North Yorkshire">North Yorkshire</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lincolnshire" title="Lincolnshire">Lincolnshire</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yorkshire_and_the_Humber_(European_Parliament_constituency)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was formed as a result of the <a href="/wiki/European_Parliamentary_Elections_Act_1999" title="European Parliamentary Elections Act 1999">European Parliamentary Elections Act 1999</a>, replacing a number of single-member constituencies. These were <a href="/wiki/Humberside_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Humberside (European Parliament constituency)">Humberside</a>, <a href="/wiki/Leeds_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Leeds (European Parliament constituency)">Leeds</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Yorkshire_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="North Yorkshire (European Parliament constituency)">North Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sheffield_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Sheffield (European Parliament constituency)">Sheffield</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_South_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Yorkshire South (European Parliament constituency)">Yorkshire South</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_South_West_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Yorkshire South West (European Parliament constituency)">Yorkshire South West</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_West_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Yorkshire West (European Parliament constituency)">Yorkshire West</a>, and parts of <a href="/wiki/Cleveland_and_Richmond_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Cleveland and Richmond (European Parliament constituency)">Cleveland and Richmond</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lincolnshire_and_Humberside_South_(European_Parliament_constituency)" title="Lincolnshire and Humberside South (European Parliament constituency)">Lincolnshire and Humberside South</a>.
</p>
