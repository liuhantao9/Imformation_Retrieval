	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Timeless Festival</h1>

		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><table class="infobox vcard plainlist" style="width:22em"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold;background-color: #b0c4de"><div style="display:inline;" class="fn org">Timeless Festival</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Origin</th><td><a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, England, UK</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Genres</th><td><a href="/wiki/Electronica" title="Electronica">Electronica</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">hip hop</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dance_music" title="Dance music">dance</a>, <a href="/wiki/Soul_music" title="Soul music">soul</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row"><span class="nowrap">Years active</span></th><td>2002–2005</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Website</th><td><a rel="nofollow" class="external free" href="http://www.timelessfestival.com/">http://www.timelessfestival.com/</a></td></tr><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;background-color: #b0c4de"></th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Members</th><td>Timeless Music Project</td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>Timeless Festival</b> was a community <a href="/wiki/Music_festival" title="Music festival">music festival</a> in <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a>, which ran from 2002 to 2005. <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i> chose the festival as its 'pick of the week' 2 years running (2004 and 2005). The festival was organised by <a href="/w/index.php?title=Chocolate_Fireguard_Records&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Chocolate Fireguard Records (page does not exist)">Chocolate Fireguard Records</a>  and the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Timeless_Music_Project&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Timeless Music Project (page does not exist)">Timeless Music Project</a> and featured three stages in Ravensknowle Park, Wakefield Road, <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>
</p><p>Acts included <a href="/wiki/DJ_Vadim" title="DJ Vadim">DJ Vadim</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lo_Fidelity_Allstars" title="Lo Fidelity Allstars">Lo Fidelity Allstars</a>, Fat City Records Sound System, <a href="/wiki/Oneself" class="mw-redirect" title="Oneself">Oneself</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zion_Train" title="Zion Train">Zion Train</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kava_Kava" class="mw-redirect" title="Kava Kava">Kava Kava</a>, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Root_Jackson&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Root Jackson (page does not exist)">Root Jackson</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Freestylers" class="mw-redirect" title="The Freestylers">The Freestylers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fingathing" title="Fingathing">Fingathing</a>, <a href="/wiki/Transglobal_Underground" title="Transglobal Underground">Transglobal Underground</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Bays" title="The Bays">The Bays</a>, Panjabi Hit Squad, La Cedille, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Snowboy_and_the_Latin_Section&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Snowboy and the Latin Section (page does not exist)">Snowboy and the Latin Section</a>, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Iration_Steppas&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Iration Steppas (page does not exist)">Iration Steppas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jehst" title="Jehst">Jehst</a>, Asaviour, <a href="/w/index.php?title=DJ_IQ&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="DJ IQ (page does not exist)">DJ IQ</a>, <a href="/wiki/Four_Day_Hombre" class="mw-redirect" title="Four Day Hombre">Four Day Hombre</a>, The Voltaires, <a href="/wiki/The_Selecter" title="The Selecter">The Selecter</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pee_Wee_Ellis" class="mw-redirect" title="Pee Wee Ellis">Pee Wee Ellis</a>, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Dub_Dadda&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Dub Dadda (page does not exist)">Dub Dadda</a> and many local acts, workshops, a freestyle hip hop stage, films, visuals from VJs, acts from <a href="/wiki/Besan%C3%A7on" title="Besançon">Besançon</a> (Huddersfield's twin town in France)
</p><p>Side projects include the regional CD sampler of <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a> based record labels for international Music Conferences, a 3 x CD Huddersfield showcase compilation Sounds From A Big Town and various other projects aimed at profiling the area c/o Timeless Music Project.
</p>
