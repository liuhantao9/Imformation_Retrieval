/>.org</a></span></td></tr></tbody></table><p>The <b>Association for Computational Linguistics</b> (<b>ACL</b>) is the international scientific and professional society for people working on problems involving <a href="/wiki/Natural_language_and_computation" class="mw-redirect" title="Natural language and computation">natural language and computation</a>. An annual meeting is held each summer in locations where significant <a href="/wiki/Computational_linguistics" title="Computational linguistics">computational linguistics</a> research is carried out. It was founded in 1962, originally named the <b>Association for Machine Translation and Computational Linguistics</b> (<b>AMTCL</b>). It became the ACL in 1968.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The ACL has a European (<a href="/w/index.php?title=European_Chapter_of_the_Association_for_Computational_Linguistics&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="European Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics (page does not exist)">EACL</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and a North American (<a href="/wiki/North_American_Chapter_of_the_Association_for_Computational_Linguistics" title="North American Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics">NAACL</a>) chapter.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The ACL journal, <a href="/wiki/Computational_Linguistics_(journal)" title="Computational Linguistics (journal)"><i>Computational Linguistics</i></a>, is the primary forum for research on computational linguistics and <a href="/wiki/Natural_language_processing" title="Natural language processing">natural language processing</a>. Since 1988, the journal has been published for the ACL by <a href="/wiki/MIT_Press" title="MIT Press">MIT Press</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
