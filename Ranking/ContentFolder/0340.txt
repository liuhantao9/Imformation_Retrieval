<p>A <b>title of honor</b> or <b>honorary title</b> is a title bestowed upon individuals or organizations as an <a href="/wiki/Award" class="mw-redirect" title="Award">award</a> in recognition of their merits. 
</p><p>Sometimes the title bears the same or nearly the same name as a <a href="/wiki/Title_of_authority" title="Title of authority">title of authority</a>, but the person bestowed does not have to carry out any duties, possibly except for ceremonial ones. In some cases, the title is bestowed posthumously.
</p><p>Some examples of honorary titles from various areas are: 
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Academician" title="Academician">Academician</a> – <a href="/wiki/Honorary_title_(academic)" title="Honorary title (academic)">Honorary title (academic)</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Colonel_(title)" title="Colonel (title)">Colonel (title)</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Fellow" title="Fellow">Fellow</a> of an academic, artistic, or professional society</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Freeman_of_the_City_of_London" class="mw-redirect" title="Freeman of the City of London">Freeman of the City of London</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hero_of_the_Russian_Federation" title="Hero of the Russian Federation">Hero of the Russian Federation</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Honorary_degree" title="Honorary degree">Honorary degree</a> or position, such as honorary Professor</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight">Knight</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dame_(title)" class="mw-redirect" title="Dame (title)">Dame</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Lady%27s_companion" title="Lady&#39;s companion">Companion</a> of an honorific order</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/New_Knowledge_Worker_of_Korea" title="New Knowledge Worker of Korea">New Knowledge Worker of Korea</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/People%27s_Artist" title="People&#39;s Artist">People's Artist</a></li>
<li>Honorary counselors (<i>neuvos</i>) in Finland, such as <a href="/wiki/Counselor_of_State_(Finland)" title="Counselor of State (Finland)">valtioneuvos</a> (Counselor of State) and <a href="/wiki/Vuorineuvos" title="Vuorineuvos">vuorineuvos</a> (Counselor of Mining)</li></ul>
<p>Some historical honorary titles may be bought, just like certain <a href="/wiki/Nobility" title="Nobility">nobility</a> titles. This has long been a matter of fraud, both outright and indirect.
</p><p>Some honorary titles serve as positions of <a href="/wiki/Sinecure" title="Sinecure">sinecure</a> and honorary <a href="/wiki/Retirement" title="Retirement">retirement</a>.
</p>
