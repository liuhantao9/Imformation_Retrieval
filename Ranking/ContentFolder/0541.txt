</p><p><b>Lowerhouses</b> is a residential district in the town of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England.
</p>
<div class="center"><div class="thumb tnone"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:502px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lowerhouses_P6030011_(RLH).JPG" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Lowerhouses_P6030011_%28RLH%29.JPG/500px-Lowerhouses_P6030011_%28RLH%29.JPG" decoding="async" width="500" height="207" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Lowerhouses_P6030011_%28RLH%29.JPG/750px-Lowerhouses_P6030011_%28RLH%29.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Lowerhouses_P6030011_%28RLH%29.JPG/1000px-Lowerhouses_P6030011_%28RLH%29.JPG 2x" data-file-width="1343" data-file-height="555" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lowerhouses_P6030011_(RLH).JPG" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Lowerhouses viewed from <a href="/wiki/Castle_Hill,_Huddersfield" title="Castle Hill, Huddersfield">Castle Hill, Huddersfield</a></div></div></div></div>
<p>Lowerhouses is a residential district situated in a wooded hillside, adjacent to <a href="/wiki/Hall_Bower" title="Hall Bower">Hall Bower</a>, between <a href="/wiki/Newsome" title="Newsome">Newsome</a> and <a href="/wiki/Almondbury" title="Almondbury">Almondbury</a>, approximately 1 mile (1.6&#160;km) of Huddersfield town centre. It comprises multiple social housing estates, with a mixture of private housing throughout. 
</p><p>It has a Primary school, two local shops and a <a href="/wiki/Take-out" title="Take-out">take-away</a>.
</p><p><br />
