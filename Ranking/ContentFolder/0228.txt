		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>This is a <b>list of nickname-related list articles on Wikipedia</b>. A <a href="/wiki/Nickname" title="Nickname">nickname</a> is "a familiar or humorous name given to a person or thing instead of or as well as the real name."<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> A nickname is often considered desirable, symbolising a form of acceptance, but can sometimes be a form of ridicule. A moniker also means a nickname or personal name. The word often distinguishes personal names from nicknames that became proper names out of former nicknames. English examples are Bob and Rob, nickname variants for Robert.
</p>
