<p><b>Waterloo</b> is a suburb of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England. It is 2 miles (3.2&#160;km) to the east of Huddersfield Town Centre. It is a part of Dalton named after the former Waterloo public house. It consists mostly of housing and has a central shopping area, with independent shops and a large <a href="/wiki/Morrisons" title="Morrisons">Morrisons</a> Supermarket and petrol station located on Penistone Road.
</p><p>Waterloo's population is around 7,000 and it is situated between <a href="/wiki/Dalton,_Huddersfield" title="Dalton, Huddersfield">Dalton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Almondbury" title="Almondbury">Almondbury</a>.
</p><p>The area is home to a number of businesses, including Forteq UK (the UK operation of a Swiss-owned <a href="/wiki/Injection_molding" class="mw-redirect" title="Injection molding">plastic moulding</a> company<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>), <a href="/wiki/Principle_Group" title="Principle Group">Principle Group</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Brand_implementation" title="Brand implementation">brand implementation</a> company that makes <a href="/wiki/Signage" title="Signage">signage</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and bank counters<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>), Hyder Living (a bed<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and sofa manufacturer) and Metal Closures (metal packaging producer).
</p>
