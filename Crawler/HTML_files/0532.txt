<p><b>Fartown</b> is a district of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> that starts 0.6 miles (1&#160;km) north of the town centre. 
</p><p>Fartown runs for approximately 1 mile (1.6&#160;km) either side of the <a href="/wiki/A641_road" class="mw-redirect" title="A641 road">A641</a> main Huddersfield to <a href="/wiki/Bradford" title="Bradford">Bradford</a> Road. The district area stretches from the top of Woodhouse Hill to the Halifax Old Road with a population of 4,735 according to the 2001 Census. 
</p><p>Fartown currently has a multi-ethnic population with a significant percentage of people hailing from South Asian and West Indian backgrounds. 
</p><p>Two major hills are also situated in Fartown, Woodhouse Hill towards <a href="/wiki/Sheepridge,_Huddersfield" title="Sheepridge, Huddersfield">Sheepridge</a> and York Avenue towards <a href="/wiki/Cowcliffe" title="Cowcliffe">Cowcliffe</a>. <a href="/wiki/North_Huddersfield_Trust_School" title="North Huddersfield Trust School">North Huddersfield Trust School</a> is situated just off Woodhouse Hill.
</p><p>Huddersfield's Rugby League club <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Giants" title="Huddersfield Giants">Huddersfield RLFC</a> played at the <a href="/wiki/Fartown_Ground" title="Fartown Ground">Fartown Ground</a> as did the <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Yorkshire County Cricket Club">Yorkshire County Cricket Club</a>. The ground there now is little used for sporting events.
</p>

<p>Fartown's name was cited as "hilarious" in the <a href="/wiki/XFM" class="mw-redirect" title="XFM">XFM</a> radio show presented by <a href="/wiki/Ricky_Gervais" title="Ricky Gervais">Ricky Gervais</a> in 2003.
</p><p>A house in Ashfield Street was used as the main family home in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">British</a> feature film <i><a href="/wiki/Between_Two_Women_(2000_film)" title="Between Two Women (2000 film)">Between Two Women</a></i>.
</p>
