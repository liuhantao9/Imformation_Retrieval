<p><b>Rohan Janse van Rensburg</b> (born 11 September 1994) is a South African <a href="/wiki/Rugby_union" title="Rugby union">rugby union</a> player for English <a href="/wiki/Premiership_Rugby" title="Premiership Rugby">Premiership</a> side  <a href="/wiki/Sale_Sharks" title="Sale Sharks">Sale Sharks</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-SARU_Player_Profile_Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SARU_Player_Profile_Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Scoresway_Player_Profile_Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Scoresway_Player_Profile_Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> His regular position is inside centre.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Youth">Youth</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Youth">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Janse van Rensburg represented the  <a href="/wiki/Blue_Bulls" title="Blue Bulls">Blue Bulls</a> at the 2007 Under-13 Craven Week, the 2010 Under-16 Grant Khomo Week and the 2011 and 2012 editions of the Under-18 <a href="/wiki/Craven_Week" title="Craven Week">Craven Week</a> tournaments. He was also called into the South African Schools squad in 2012.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Senior_career">Senior career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Senior career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>His senior debut for the  <a href="/wiki/Blue_Bulls" title="Blue Bulls">Blue Bulls</a> came in the <a href="/wiki/2013_Vodacom_Cup" title="2013 Vodacom Cup">2013 Vodacom Cup</a> competition, when he came on as a substitute in the match against the  <a href="/wiki/Pumas_(rugby_team)" title="Pumas (rugby team)">Pumas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Vodacom_Blue_Bulls_42-44_Steval_Pumas_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Vodacom_Blue_Bulls_42-44_Steval_Pumas-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He then started five of their remaining six matches in this competition.
</p><p>Janse van Rensburg moved to <a href="/wiki/Johannesburg" title="Johannesburg">Johannesburg</a> for the 2015 to join the  <a href="/wiki/Golden_Lions" title="Golden Lions">Golden Lions</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Lions_snatch_up_four_Bulls_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Lions_snatch_up_four_Bulls-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2016_season">2016 season</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2016 season">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/2016_Super_Rugby_season" title="2016 Super Rugby season">2016 Super Rugby season</a> was the breakthrough for Janse van Rensburg. At inside centre,he had a very successful partnership with fellow centre, Springbok <a href="/wiki/Lionel_Mapoe" title="Lionel Mapoe">Lionel Mapoe</a>. Janse van Rensburg managed to score 10 tries in 17 games;which is very prolific for a number 12. The Golden Lions managed to make the <a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_Super_Rugby_final&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2016 Super Rugby final (page does not exist)">2016 Super Rugby final</a> but lost to the <a href="/wiki/Hurricanes_(rugby_union)" title="Hurricanes (rugby union)">Hurricanes</a> 20-3.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Sale_Sharks">Sale Sharks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Sale Sharks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Janse van Rensburg made eight appearances for English <a href="/wiki/Premiership_Rugby" title="Premiership Rugby">Premiership</a> side  <a href="/wiki/Sale_Sharks" title="Sale Sharks">Sale Sharks</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2017%E2%80%9318_English_Premiership" class="mw-redirect" title="2017–18 English Premiership">2017–18 English Premiership</a> during a loan spell in 2018, before returning on a permanent deal before the <a href="/wiki/2018%E2%80%9319_English_Premiership" class="mw-redirect" title="2018–19 English Premiership">2018–19 season</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Representative_rugby">Representative rugby</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Representative rugby">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Janse van Rensburg was included in the S.A. Schools squad in 2012 and the <a href="/wiki/South_Africa_national_under-20_rugby_union_team" title="South Africa national under-20 rugby union team">South Africa Under-20</a> squad for the <a href="/wiki/2013_IRB_Junior_World_Championship" title="2013 IRB Junior World Championship">2013</a><sup id="cite_ref-Steenkamp_leads_powerful_SA_U20_JWC_squad_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Steenkamp_leads_powerful_SA_U20_JWC_squad-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/2014_IRB_Junior_World_Championship" title="2014 IRB Junior World Championship">2014</a><sup id="cite_ref-Junior_Springboks_named_for_JWC_2014_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Junior_Springboks_named_for_JWC_2014-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/IRB_Junior_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="IRB Junior World Championship">IRB Junior World Championship</a> tournaments.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Springboks">Springboks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rohan_Janse_van_Rensburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Springboks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Janse van Rensburg first represented <a href="/w/index.php?title=Springboks_XV&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Springboks XV (page does not exist)">Springboks XV</a> in a non-cap friendly vs <a href="/w/index.php?title=Barbarians_XV&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Barbarians XV (page does not exist)">Barbarians XV</a> in which he scored a last-gasp try to secure a 31-31 draw. He was later included in the <a href="/wiki/Springboks" class="mw-redirect" title="Springboks">Springboks</a> squad for their 2016 Outgoing series of Europe. He started the final game of the tour vs <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a> at inside centre where the Springboks lost.
</p>
