<p><b>Scholes</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Pennines" title="Pennines">Pennine</a> hilltop village, situated  1&#160;mile (2&#160;km) to the southeast and above <a href="/wiki/Holmfirth" title="Holmfirth">Holmfirth</a>, 7&#160;miles (11&#160;km) southwest of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Holme_Valley" title="Holme Valley">Holme Valley</a> of <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, England. It has a population of 1,990.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The name <i>Scholes</i> may have originated from the <a href="/wiki/Scandinavia" title="Scandinavia">Scandinavian</a> language meaning 'the temporary huts or sheds'.
</p><p>The village contains one non denominational <a href="/wiki/Primary_school" title="Primary school">primary school</a>, originally built in 1908, modernised in 1976 and extended in 1986. The school caters for approximately 213 pupils aged four to eleven.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Scholes was the birthplace of the <a href="/wiki/Entertainer" class="mw-redirect" title="Entertainer">entertainer</a> <a href="/wiki/Roy_Castle" title="Roy Castle">Roy Castle</a>, well known as the <a href="/wiki/Television_presenter" title="Television presenter">presenter</a> of the long-running BBC show <i><a href="/wiki/Record_Breakers" title="Record Breakers">Record Breakers</a></i>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Local_sports">Local sports</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scholes,_Holme_Valley&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Local sports">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Scholes has a successful cricket team in the <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Cricket_League" title="Huddersfield Cricket League">Drakes Huddersfield League</a> and teams in the Huddersfield &amp; District FA competition.
