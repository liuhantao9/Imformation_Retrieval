<p><b>Gabriella Zanna Vanessa Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe</b> (born 8 April 1989),<sup id="cite_ref-FMD_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FMD-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> known professionally as <b>Gabriella Wilde</b> or <b>Gabriella Calthorpe</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-FMD_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FMD-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> is an English model and actress who has appeared in the films <i><a href="/wiki/St_Trinian%27s_2:_The_Legend_of_Fritton%27s_Gold" title="St Trinian&#39;s 2: The Legend of Fritton&#39;s Gold">St Trinian's 2: The Legend of Fritton's Gold</a></i> (2009), <i><a href="/wiki/The_Three_Musketeers_(2011_film)" title="The Three Musketeers (2011 film)">The Three Musketeers</a></i> (2011), <i><a href="/wiki/Carrie_(2013_film)" title="Carrie (2013 film)">Carrie</a></i> (2013) and <i><a href="/wiki/Endless_Love_(2014_film)" title="Endless Love (2014 film)">Endless Love</a></i> (2014). Her television work includes the <i><a href="/wiki/Doctor_Who" title="Doctor Who">Doctor Who</a></i> episode "<a href="/wiki/The_Vampires_of_Venice" title="The Vampires of Venice">The Vampires of Venice</a>" (2010) and the <a href="/wiki/BBC" title="BBC">BBC</a> historical period drama series <i><a href="/wiki/Poldark_(2015_TV_series)" title="Poldark (2015 TV series)">Poldark</a></i> (2016–present).<sup id="cite_ref-tatler_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tatler-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabriella_Wilde&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Wilde was born in <a href="/wiki/Basingstoke" title="Basingstoke">Basingstoke</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hampshire" title="Hampshire">Hampshire</a>, England. She is descended from the aristocratic <a href="/wiki/Gough-Calthorpe_family" title="Gough-Calthorpe family">Gough-Calthorpe family</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ThisLondon_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ThisLondon-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Her father, businessman <a href="/wiki/John_Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe" title="John Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe">John Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe</a>, is a former chairman of the Watermark Group, and the grandson of <a href="/wiki/Fitzroy_Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe" title="Fitzroy Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe">Sir Fitzroy Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe, 1st Baronet</a>. Her mother, Vanessa Mary Theresa (née Hubbard), is the former wife of socialite <a href="/wiki/Dai_Llewellyn" title="Dai Llewellyn">Sir Dai Llewellyn, 4th Baronet</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DailyMail_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DailyMail-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Vanessa is a former model who sat for <a href="/wiki/David_Bailey_(photographer)" class="mw-redirect" title="David Bailey (photographer)">David Bailey</a> and <a href="/wiki/John_Swannell_(photographer)" title="John Swannell (photographer)">John Swannell</a>.
</p><p>Through her maternal grandfather, Wilde's ancestors include <a href="/wiki/Montagu_Bertie,_6th_Earl_of_Abingdon" title="Montagu Bertie, 6th Earl of Abingdon">Montagu Bertie, 6th Earl of Abingdon</a>, General the Hon. <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Gage" title="Thomas Gage">Thomas Gage</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Stephanus_Van_Cortlandt" title="Stephanus Van Cortlandt">Stephanus Van Cortlandt</a>, the first native-born Mayor of <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>. Wilde's maternal grandmother's parents were <a href="/wiki/Peerage" title="Peerage">peers</a> <a href="/wiki/Bernard_Fitzalan-Howard,_3rd_Baron_Howard_of_Glossop" title="Bernard Fitzalan-Howard, 3rd Baron Howard of Glossop">Bernard Fitzalan-Howard, 3rd Baron Howard of Glossop</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mona_Fitzalan-Howard,_11th_Baroness_Beaumont" title="Mona Fitzalan-Howard, 11th Baroness Beaumont">Mona Fitzalan-Howard, 11th Baroness Beaumont</a>.
</p><p>Wilde has a younger sister, Octavia, as well as five half-siblings: <a href="/wiki/Olivia_Llewellyn" title="Olivia Llewellyn">Olivia</a> and Arabella Llewellyn, from her mother's first marriage, and Georgiana, <a href="/wiki/Isabella_Calthorpe" title="Isabella Calthorpe">Isabella</a>, and Jacobi, from her father's first marriage, to Lady Mary-Gaye Curzon.<sup id="cite_ref-tatler_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tatler-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> She is also "unofficial stepsisters" with Pandora Cooper-Key and <a href="/wiki/Cressida_Bonas" title="Cressida Bonas">Cressida Bonas</a>, Lady Mary-Gaye's other daughters. Isabella, Olivia and Cressida are also actresses.<sup id="cite_ref-calthorpe_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-calthorpe-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Wilde and her future husband were both educated at <a href="/wiki/Windlesham_House_School" title="Windlesham House School">Windlesham House School</a>. Afterwards she attended <a href="/wiki/Heathfield_School,_Ascot" title="Heathfield School, Ascot">Heathfield St Mary's School</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ascot,_Berkshire" title="Ascot, Berkshire">Ascot</a> and <a href="/wiki/St_Swithun%27s_School,_Winchester" title="St Swithun&#39;s School, Winchester">St Swithun's School, Winchester</a>, before leaving to pursue a course in art while continuing with her modelling career.<sup id="cite_ref-ThisLondon_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ThisLondon-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She smuggled vodka into Heathfield, resulting in suspension and a transfer to St Swithun's.<sup id="cite_ref-ThisLondon_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ThisLondon-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She studied <a href="/wiki/Fine_art" title="Fine art">fine art</a> at the <a href="/wiki/City_and_Guilds_of_London_Art_School" title="City and Guilds of London Art School">City and Guilds of London Art School</a> but dropped out to pursue acting.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabriella_Wilde&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:216px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Gabrielle_Wilde_in_Three_Musketeers.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5b/Gabrielle_Wilde_in_Three_Musketeers.jpg" decoding="async" width="214" height="296" class="thumbimage" data-file-width="214" data-file-height="296" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Gabrielle_Wilde_in_Three_Musketeers.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Wilde in 2010, filming a scene for <i><a href="/wiki/The_Three_Musketeers_(2011_film)" title="The Three Musketeers (2011 film)">The Three Musketeers</a></i></div></div></div>
<p>Wilde began her career as a model. Spotted at age 14 by <a href="/wiki/Naomi_Campbell" title="Naomi Campbell">Naomi Campbell</a>, she joined her agency <a href="/w/index.php?title=Premier_Model_Management&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Premier Model Management (page does not exist)">Premier Model Management</a> and appeared in campaigns for companies including <a href="/wiki/L.K.Bennett" title="L.K.Bennett">L.K.Bennett</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lacoste" title="Lacoste">Lacoste</a>, <a href="/wiki/Abercrombie_%26_Fitch" title="Abercrombie &amp; Fitch">Abercrombie &amp; Fitch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Burberry" title="Burberry">Burberry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Topshop" title="Topshop">Topshop</a> and posed for <i><a href="/wiki/InStyle" title="InStyle">InStyle</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Cosmopolitan_(magazine)" title="Cosmopolitan (magazine)">Cosmopolitan</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Nylon_(magazine)" title="Nylon (magazine)">Nylon</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2007, she was named the second-most-eligible girl in Britain but rejected press attention and the "<a href="/wiki/It_girl" title="It girl">It girl</a>" label. She did not do her first interview until 2008, when she said: "I don't understand why I got anywhere near such a list because I have done nothing to help it. It's the same with my sister Isabella – we both avoid the society thing. We never go to parties, we never hang out in the clubs associated with all that – we are incredibly private."<sup id="cite_ref-calthorpe_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-calthorpe-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In December 2009, Wilde made her acting debut in the adventure comedy film <i><a href="/wiki/St_Trinian%27s_2:_The_Legend_of_Fritton%27s_Gold" title="St Trinian&#39;s 2: The Legend of Fritton&#39;s Gold">St Trinian's 2: The Legend of Fritton's Gold</a></i>. In May 2010, Wilde appeared in an episode of the BBC television series <i><a href="/wiki/Doctor_Who" title="Doctor Who">Doctor Who</a></i> as one of the eponymous vampires in "<a href="/wiki/The_Vampires_of_Venice" title="The Vampires of Venice">The Vampires of Venice</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In December 2010, Wilde was announced to have been cast in her film debut in the adventure film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Three_Musketeers_(2011_film)" title="The Three Musketeers (2011 film)">The Three Musketeers</a></i>, an adaptation of the novel of the same name. Wilde portrayed the role of the queen's lady-in-waiting Constance Bonacieux and starred alongside <a href="/wiki/Orlando_Bloom" title="Orlando Bloom">Orlando Bloom</a> and <a href="/wiki/Logan_Lerman" title="Logan Lerman">Logan Lerman</a>. The film was released in October 2011 and went on to make over $132 million worldwide.<sup id="cite_ref-BOM_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BOM-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> The film was poorly received by critics with a rating of 24% on <a href="/wiki/Rotten_Tomatoes" title="Rotten Tomatoes">Rotten Tomatoes</a> based on reviews from 90 critics.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In March 2012, Wilde was cast in the <a href="/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company" title="American Broadcasting Company">ABC</a> Sci-Fi television pilot <i>Dark Horse</i>. The series centers on a science student who learns that he is the chosen one, tasked with destroying the forces of evil. The pilot was not picked up by ABC for the 2012–2013 television season.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> That same year Wilde starred in the short film <i>Il Maestro</i> and is attached to another film titled <i>Squatters</i>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2017)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p><p>As well as <i>Dark Horse</i>, Wilde was cast in the 2013 remake, <i><a href="/wiki/Carrie_(2013_film)" title="Carrie (2013 film)">Carrie</a></i>, that same month (as <a href="/wiki/Sue_Snell" title="Sue Snell">Sue Snell</a>). <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabriella_Wilde&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Wilde married musician <a href="/wiki/Alan_Pownall" title="Alan Pownall">Alan Pownall</a> on 13 September 2014, both previously having attended Windlesham House School.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> She had given birth to their son Sasha Blue Pownall on 3 February 2014.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> In 2016, Wilde gave birth to their second son, Shiloh Silva Pownall.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> She and her husband are currently expecting their third child.
