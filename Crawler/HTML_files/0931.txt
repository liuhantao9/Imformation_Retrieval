<p>The following is a <b>list of monastic houses in <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a></b>, England.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Alien_house" class="mw-redirect" title="Alien house">Alien houses</a> are included, as are smaller establishments such as <a href="/wiki/Monastic_cell" title="Monastic cell">cells</a> and notable <a href="/wiki/Monastic_grange" title="Monastic grange">monastic granges</a> (particularly those with resident monks), and also camerae of the <a href="/wiki/Military_order_(monastic_society)" class="mw-redirect" title="Military order (monastic society)">military orders</a> of monks (<a href="/wiki/Knights_Templars" class="mw-redirect" title="Knights Templars">Knights Templars</a> and <a href="/wiki/Knights_Hospitaller" title="Knights Hospitaller">Knights Hospitaller</a>).  The numerous <a href="/wiki/Monastic_hospital" class="mw-redirect" title="Monastic hospital">monastic hospitals</a> <i>per se</i> are not included here unless at some time the foundation had, or was purported to have the status or function of an <a href="/wiki/Abbey" title="Abbey">abbey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Priory" title="Priory">priory</a>, <a href="/wiki/Friary" class="mw-redirect" title="Friary">friary</a> or <a href="/wiki/Preceptor#Christian_military_orders" title="Preceptor">preceptor</a>/commandery.
</p><p>The name of the county is given where there is reference to an establishment in another county.  Where the county has changed since the foundation's dissolution the modern county is given in parentheses, and in instances where the referenced foundation ceased to exist before the unification of England, the kingdom is given, followed by the modern county in parentheses.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Abbreviations_and_key">Abbreviations and key</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template:MonasticHouses_Abbreviations%26Key_England&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-1" title="Edit section: ">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<table class="wikitable">
<caption>Sites listed are ruins unless indicated
</caption>
<tbody><tr>
<th>*
</th>
<td>current monastic function
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th><sup>+</sup>
</th>
<td>current non-monastic ecclesiastic function (including remains incorporated into later structure)
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th>^
</th>
<td>current non-ecclesiastic function (including remains incorporated into later structure) or redundant intact structure
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th><sup>$</sup>
</th>
<td>remains limited to earthworks etc.
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th><sup>#</sup>
</th>
<td>no identifiable trace of the monastic foundation remains
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th><sup>~</sup>
</th>
<td>indicates exact site of monastic foundation unknown
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th><sup>≈</sup>
</th>
<td>identification ambiguous or confused
</td></tr></tbody></table>
<p>Locations with names in <i>italics</i> indicate probable duplication (misidentification with another location) or non-existent foundations (either erroneous reference or proposed foundation never implemented) or ecclesiastical establishments with a monastic appellation but lacking monastic connection.
