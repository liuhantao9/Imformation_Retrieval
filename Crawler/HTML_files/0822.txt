	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Keith Buckley (actor)</h1>

		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Keith Buckley</b> (born 7 April 1941 in <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Riding_of_Yorkshire" title="West Riding of Yorkshire">West Riding of Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>) is a British actor who has mostly appeared on television and films since 1958.
He showed his talent for acting in school plays at <a href="/w/index.php?title=Huddersfield_College_Grammar_School&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Huddersfield College Grammar School (page does not exist)">Huddersfield College Grammar School</a>.
Buckley has made many appearances in film and television appearing in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Avengers_(TV_series)" title="The Avengers (TV series)">The Avengers</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Randall_and_Hopkirk_(Deceased)" title="Randall and Hopkirk (Deceased)">Randall and Hopkirk</a></i> in the 1960s and <i><a href="/wiki/The_New_Avengers_(TV_series)" title="The New Avengers (TV series)">The New Avengers</a></i> in the 1970s.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
