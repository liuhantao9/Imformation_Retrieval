<p><b>Plunkett-Ernle-Erle-Drax</b> is the <a href="/wiki/Double-barrelled_name" title="Double-barrelled name">quadruple-barrelled</a> surname of the descendants of Admiral The Honourable Sir <a href="/wiki/Reginald_Drax" title="Reginald Drax">Reginald Aylmer Ranfurly Plunkett-Ernle-Erle-Drax</a> (1880–1967), who was the younger son of <a href="/wiki/John_Plunkett,_17th_Baron_of_Dunsany" title="John Plunkett, 17th Baron of Dunsany">John William Plunkett</a>, 17th <a href="/wiki/Baron_of_Dunsany" title="Baron of Dunsany">Baron of Dunsany</a> by his wife Ernle Elizabeth Louisa Maria Grosvenor, née Burton, later <a href="/wiki/Ernle" title="Ernle">Ernle</a>-Erle-Drax (1855–1916). The surname of Plunkett-Ernle-Erle-Drax was assumed in lieu of Plunkett, his name from birth, by <a href="/w/index.php?title=Royal_licence&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Royal licence (page does not exist)">royal licence</a> on 4 October 1916. Ernle is pronounced <i>earnly</i>. The current head of the family is the Westminster M.P. <a href="/wiki/Richard_Grosvenor_Plunkett-Ernle-Erle-Drax" class="mw-redirect" title="Richard Grosvenor Plunkett-Ernle-Erle-Drax">Richard Grosvenor Plunkett-Ernle-Erle-Drax</a>, otherwise <a href="/wiki/Richard_Drax" title="Richard Drax">Richard Drax</a>.
</p><p>See <a href="/wiki/Burke%27s_Peerage_and_Baronetage" class="mw-redirect" title="Burke&#39;s Peerage and Baronetage">Burke's Peerage and Baronetage</a> under the account of the <a href="/wiki/Baron_Dunsany" class="mw-redirect" title="Baron Dunsany">Dunsany barony</a> for a complete listing of the Plunkett-Ernle-Erle-Drax family.
</p>
