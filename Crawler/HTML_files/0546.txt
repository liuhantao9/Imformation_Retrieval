<p><br />
The <b>South and West Yorkshire Green Belt</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Green_belt" title="Green belt">green belt</a> <a href="/wiki/Environmental_policy" title="Environmental policy">environmental</a> and <a href="/wiki/Planning_in_the_United_Kingdom" class="mw-redirect" title="Planning in the United Kingdom">planning</a> policy that regulates the rural space within the <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_and_the_Humber" title="Yorkshire and the Humber">Yorkshire and the Humber</a> region of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. It is contained within the counties of <a href="/wiki/South_Yorkshire" title="South Yorkshire">South Yorkshire</a> and <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Yorkshire" title="North Yorkshire">North Yorkshire</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Derbyshire" title="Derbyshire">Derbyshire</a>. Essentially, its primary function is to more rigorously manage development around the cities, towns and villages in the large <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire_Urban_Area" title="West Yorkshire Urban Area">West Yorkshire Urban Area</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>-<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>, the <a href="/wiki/Sheffield_urban_area" title="Sheffield urban area">Sheffield urban area</a><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> and surrounding towns of <a href="/wiki/Barnsley" title="Barnsley">Barnsley</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Doncaster" title="Doncaster">Doncaster</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Rotherham" title="Rotherham">Rotherham</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> as well as other nearby locations,<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> in order to discourage <a href="/wiki/Urban_sprawl" title="Urban sprawl">urban sprawl</a> and further convergence between these.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> It is managed by <a href="/wiki/Local_planning_authority_(UK)" class="mw-redirect" title="Local planning authority (UK)">local planning authorities</a> on guidance from central government.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=South_and_West_Yorkshire_Green_Belt&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Land area total of the green belt is 248,241 hectares (2,482.41&#160;km<sup>2</sup>; 958.46&#160;sq&#160;mi) (1.9% of the total land area of England (2010)<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>. The vast coverage is within the South and West Yorkshire counties, with extensions towards <a href="/wiki/Harrogate" title="Harrogate">Harrogate</a> and <a href="/wiki/Knaresborough" title="Knaresborough">Knaresborough</a> in the south of the North Yorkshire county, and <a href="/wiki/Chesterfield" title="Chesterfield">Chesterfield</a> in north eastern Derbyshire.
</p><p>The South and West Yorkshire Green Belt is contiguous with the <a href="/wiki/North_West_Green_Belt" title="North West Green Belt">North West Green Belt</a> along the <a href="/wiki/Greater_Manchester" title="Greater Manchester">Greater Manchester</a> and West Yorkshire county boundary, creating a continuous band of protected area through to the English western coastline by the <a href="/wiki/River_Mersey" title="River Mersey">River Mersey</a> and <a href="/wiki/Irish_Sea" title="Irish Sea">Irish Sea</a>. The City of <a href="/wiki/York_Green_Belt" title="York Green Belt">York Green Belt</a> is also a few miles to the north east.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Peak_District_National_Park" class="mw-redirect" title="Peak District National Park">Peak District National Park</a> lies between the green belt and the <a href="/wiki/Stoke-on-Trent_Green_Belt" title="Stoke-on-Trent Green Belt">Stoke-on-Trent Green Belt</a> to the southwest, while <a href="/wiki/Nottingham_and_Derby_Green_Belt" title="Nottingham and Derby Green Belt">Nottingham</a>'s green belt is to the south. As the green belt lies across several counties, responsibility and co-ordination rests with the many local <a href="/wiki/District_council_(England)" class="mw-redirect" title="District council (England)">district councils</a> whose land covers the belt, as these are the local planning government bodies. 
</p>
