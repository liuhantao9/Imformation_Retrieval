	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Thornton Lodge</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>

<b>Thornton Lodge</b> is a suburb of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>. It borders <a href="/wiki/Crosland_Moor" title="Crosland Moor">Crosland Moor</a> to the west, <a href="/wiki/Lockwood,_West_Yorkshire" class="mw-redirect" title="Lockwood, West Yorkshire">Lockwood</a> to the south, and <a href="/wiki/Longroyd_Bridge" title="Longroyd Bridge">Longroyd Bridge</a> to the north.  It mostly comprises suburban housing and some small shops, with industrial units around the area bordering Longroyd Bridge.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Thornton_Lodge&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The housing area lies approximately 1 mile (1.6&#160;km) southwest of Huddersfield town centre, on the southern slope of the <a href="/wiki/Colne_Valley" title="Colne Valley">Colne Valley</a>'s eastern end, overlooking the <a href="/wiki/A62_road" title="A62 road">A62</a> <a href="/wiki/Leeds" title="Leeds">Leeds</a> to <a href="/wiki/Manchester" title="Manchester">Manchester</a> road.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Thornton_Lodge&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Thornton Lodge was originally a country manor that belonged to the Thornton family of Yorkshire who had owned properties in the area since at least 1686.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Community">Community</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Thornton_Lodge&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Community">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The modern population is primarily composed of second- and third-generation <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistani</a> families.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Infrastructure">Infrastructure</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Thornton_Lodge&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Infrastructure">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The major employers within this area are involved in the <a href="/wiki/Clothing" title="Clothing">clothing</a>, <a href="/wiki/Textile" title="Textile">textile</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">engineering</a> industries.
</p><p>The nearest rail services are available at <a href="/wiki/Lockwood_railway_station" title="Lockwood railway station">Lockwood railway station</a>.
</p>
