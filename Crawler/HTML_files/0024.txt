	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">European Coordinating Committee for Artificial Intelligence</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"><span class="mw-redirectedfrom">&#160;&#160;(Redirected from <a href="/w/index.php?title=ECCAI&amp;redirect=no" class="mw-redirect" title="ECCAI">ECCAI</a>)</span></div>

		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>European Association for Artificial Intelligence</b> (<b>EurAI</b>) (formerly <b>European Co-ordinating Committee for Artificial Intelligence</b> (<b>ECCAI</b>)) is the representative body for the <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">European</a> <a href="/wiki/Artificial_intelligence" title="Artificial intelligence">artificial intelligence</a> community. Its aim is to promote study, <a href="/wiki/Research" title="Research">research</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Application_software" title="Application software">applications</a> of artificial intelligence (AI) in Europe. It was established in 1982. 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Activities">Activities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=European_Coordinating_Committee_for_Artificial_Intelligence&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Activities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Every even-numbered year, EurAI, jointly with one of the member <a href="/wiki/Professional_association" title="Professional association">associations</a> of EurAI, holds the <a href="/wiki/European_Conference_on_Artificial_Intelligence" title="European Conference on Artificial Intelligence">European Conference on Artificial Intelligence</a> (ECAI).  The conference has become the leading conference for this field in Europe. The list of ECAI proceedings is available <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20070929002117/http://www.eccai.org/ecai.shtml">here</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Fellowship">Fellowship</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=European_Coordinating_Committee_for_Artificial_Intelligence&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Fellowship">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The  <b>EurAI Fellows</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> program has been started in 1999 to recognise individuals who have made significant, sustained contributions to the field of artificial intelligence (AI) in Europe. Fellows' accomplishments range from pioneering advances in the theory of AI, to unusual accomplishments in AI technology and applications. Usually only individuals who have made contributions to AI for a decade or more after receiving their Ph.D. (or are at an equivalent career stage) will be selected. Leadership in EurAI or EurAI member societies, support of forums for the exchange of ideas, and extended service for the international AI community also play a role in the selection process. Evidence of technical contribution will often be in the form of publications, but other evidence will also be considered, such as patent awards or statements of longstanding contribution to an industrial group effort. The EurAI Fellows Program honours only a very small percentage of the total membership of all ECCAI member societies (up to a maximum of 3%).
</p><p>The policies governing the operation of the Fellows Committee are under the review of the EurAI Board.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Dissertation_Award">Dissertation Award</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=European_Coordinating_Committee_for_Artificial_Intelligence&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Dissertation Award">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <b>Artificial Intelligence Dissertation Award</b> sponsored by EurAI has been awarded since 1998.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Member_Societies">Member Societies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=European_Coordinating_Committee_for_Artificial_Intelligence&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Member Societies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There is a <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.eurai.org/organisation/member-societies">collection of member societies</a> which are national AI societies in European <a href="/wiki/Countries" class="mw-redirect" title="Countries">countries</a>.
</p>
