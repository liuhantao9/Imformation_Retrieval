	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Timeline of the John F. Kennedy presidency</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"><span class="mw-redirectedfrom">&#160;&#160;(Redirected from <a href="/w/index.php?title=Timeline_of_the_presidency_of_John_F._Kennedy&amp;redirect=no" class="mw-redirect" title="Timeline of the presidency of John F. <p><a href="/wiki/John_F._Kennedy" title="John F. Kennedy">John F. Kennedy</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Party_(United_States)" title="Democratic Party (United States)">Democrat</a> from <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts" title="Massachusetts">Massachusetts</a>, was elected <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_United_States" title="President of the United States">President of the United States</a> on <a href="/wiki/1960_United_States_presidential_election" title="1960 United States presidential election">November 8, 1960</a> and was <a href="/wiki/Inauguration_of_John_F._Kennedy" title="Inauguration of John F. Kennedy">inaugurated</a> as the nation's <a href="/wiki/List_of_Presidents_of_the_United_States" class="mw-redirect" title="List of Presidents of the United States">35th</a> president on January 20, 1961, and ended on November 22, 1963, upon his <a href="/wiki/Assassination_of_John_F._Kennedy" title="Assassination of John F. Kennedy">assassination and death</a>. The following articles cover the timeline of <a href="/wiki/Presidency_of_John_F._Kennedy" title="Presidency of John F. Kennedy">Kennedy's presidency</a>:
