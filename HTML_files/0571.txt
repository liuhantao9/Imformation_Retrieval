<p><b>St Patrick's Church</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic" class="mw-redirect" title="Roman Catholic">Roman Catholic</a> <a href="/wiki/Parish_church" title="Parish church">Parish church</a> in Huddersfield, West Yorkshire. It was built in 1832 and was designed by Joseph Kaye, an architect from Bradford. It is situated on the junction between New North Road and <a href="/wiki/A62_road" title="A62 road">Castlegate</a> in the centre of the town. It is a Grade II listed building.<sup id="cite_ref-list_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-list-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St_Patrick%27s_Church,_Huddersfield&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Construction">Construction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St_Patrick%27s_Church,_Huddersfield&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Construction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 1831, land for the church was leased from a local family, the Ramsdens. Construction began that year and the church was designed by Joseph Kaye of Huddersfield (1783-1858). On 26 September 1832, the church was opened.<sup id="cite_ref-stock_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-stock-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Decoration">Decoration</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St_Patrick%27s_Church,_Huddersfield&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Decoration">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 1874, the interior was redecorated. That year, a new stained-glass windows was installed in the <a href="/wiki/Chancel" title="Chancel">sanctuary</a> of the church. In 1889, new seating was put into the church as well as a new organ.<sup id="cite_ref-stock_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-stock-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 1962, reordering of the church took place. A new porch was added, and a new altar and reredos were installed. More reordering followed with the side altars being later removed and lectern and font being added. From 1980 to 1981, the exterior of the church was cleaned.<sup id="cite_ref-stock_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-stock-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Parish">Parish</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St_Patrick%27s_Church,_Huddersfield&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Parish">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2007, the <a href="/wiki/Parish" title="Parish">parishes</a> of St Patrick's, Holy Family in the west and Our Lady of Lourdes in the east of the town, together with those of Saint James and Saint Brigid (which were closed) were merged to become the Parish of the Holy Redeemer.
</p><p>There are four Sunday <a href="/wiki/Mass_(Catholic_Church)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mass (Catholic Church)">Masses</a> at St Patrick's Church: 6&#160;pm on Saturday evening and 9&#160;am, 11&#160;am and 5&#160;pm on Sunday. Our Lady of Lourdes Church has two Sunday Masses: 6&#160;pm on Saturday evening and 10:30&#160;am on Sunday morning. Holy Family Church has one Sunday Mass at 9:30&#160;am.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
