<p>"<b>Good Girl</b>" is a song by American country music recording artist <a href="/wiki/Carrie_Underwood" title="Carrie Underwood">Carrie Underwood</a>. The song was written by <a href="/wiki/Chris_DeStefano" title="Chris DeStefano">Chris DeStefano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ashley_Gorley" title="Ashley Gorley">Ashley Gorley</a>, and Underwood. It was released by <a href="/wiki/Arista_Nashville" title="Arista Nashville">Arista Nashville</a> as the lead single from Underwood's fourth studio album, <i><a href="/wiki/Blown_Away_(album)" title="Blown Away (album)">Blown Away</a></i> (2012).
</p><p>Underwood debuted the song in a live performance during the annual <a href="/wiki/Country_Radio_Seminar" class="mw-redirect" title="Country Radio Seminar">Country Radio Seminar (CRS)</a> event in <a href="/wiki/Nashville" class="mw-redirect" title="Nashville">Nashville</a> on February 23, 2012. The song was sent to country radio stations directly following the performance. It was made available for purchase as a digital download on February 23, 2012. "Good Girl" officially impacted country radio on February 27, 2012 and was officially released to Hot AC radio on April 23, 2012.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Good_Girl_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On January 25, 2012, Underwood announced on her official website that the new single will be released in February. She co-wrote the song with Chris DeStefano and Ashley Gorley. Gorley has previously worked with Underwood on her singles "<a href="/wiki/Don%27t_Forget_to_Remember_Me" title="Don&#39;t Forget to Remember Me">Don't Forget to Remember Me</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/All-American_Girl_(song)" title="All-American Girl (song)">All-American Girl</a>."
</p>

<p>"Good Girl" is about Underwood warning a good girl about her ex-boyfriend, saying that he is actually no good, and that she is better off without him.
</p><p>The song is in <a href="/wiki/C_minor" title="C minor">C minor</a>, with an approximate tempo of 126 <a href="/wiki/Beats_per_minute" class="mw-redirect" title="Beats per minute">beats per minute</a>. It does not use full chords, but rather a pattern of <a href="/wiki/Perfect_fifth" title="Perfect fifth">open fifths</a> consisting of C5-F5-E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span class="music-flat">&#x266d;</span></span>5-B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span class="music-flat">&#x266d;</span></span>5. Underwood's vocal ranges from G3 to F5.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div style="clear:both;"></div>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Critical_reception">Critical reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Good_Girl_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Critical reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Upon its release, "Good Girl" received general acclaim. Roughstock gave the song four stars and praised that "this is the best uptempo vocal that Carrie Underwood has given country radio.".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Bill Lamb of About.com gave the song four out of five stars, noting that the song "crackles with rock guitar and vocal fireworks while encouraging audience participation with a singalong chorus that is pure pop."<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Bill Dukes of Taste of Country rated the song four and a half stars, saying, "By the third spin her confidence, energy and sheer talent will seduce you into believing every eight-note couldn’t be more perfectly placed."<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Kevin John Coyne of Country Universe gave the song a grade A and called it "an absolutely exhilarating record".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Good_Girl_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The short preview of the music video premiered on <i><a href="/wiki/Entertainment_Tonight" title="Entertainment Tonight">Entertainment Tonight</a></i> on March 12, 2012. Later that night, the full video was released on Underwood's official <a href="/wiki/Vevo" title="Vevo">Vevo</a> page. It features Underwood playing a "Good Girl", and another girl, trying to persuade her alter ego that the man she is with is no good.
</p><p>The music video for "Good Girl" won <i>Video of the Year</i> at the 2012 <a href="/wiki/CMT_Music_Awards" title="CMT Music Awards">CMT Music Awards</a> as voted by fans. It was directed by Theresa Wingert and edited by Jacquelyn London.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commercial_performance">Commercial performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Good_Girl_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Commercial performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Good Girl" was released for purchase on iTunes at 10pm on February 23, 2012. It sold 108,000 digital downloads in its first 3 days of sales, making it Underwood's fastest selling single ever.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> As of December 4, 2012, "Good Girl" has been certified Platinum in Canada, with more than 80,000 digital copies being sold.<sup id="cite_ref-CanadaCarrie_UnderwoodGood_GirlsingleCertRef_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CanadaCarrie_UnderwoodGood_GirlsingleCertRef-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> As of October 2015, "Good Girl" has sold 2 million copies in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Chart_performance">Chart performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Good_Girl_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Chart performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>With only 3 days of airplay, "Good Girl" debuted at number 30 on <i>Billboard</i> <a href="/wiki/Hot_Country_Songs" title="Hot Country Songs">Hot Country Songs</a> and debuted on the <i>Billboard</i> <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100">Hot 100</a> at number 24. It peaked at number 18 on the <i>Billboard</i> Hot 100, giving Underwood her twelfth top 20 on the chart. It also debuted at number 33 on the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Hot_100" title="Canadian Hot 100">Canadian Hot 100</a> chart for the week of March 10, 2012. The song made its debut on the UK Radio Airplay chart the week beginning April 1. It was the highest new entry that week, debuting at 56.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> "Good Girl" hit number one on <a href="/wiki/Hot_Country_Songs" title="Hot Country Songs">Hot Country Songs</a>, becoming Underwood's twelfth number one hit on the chart. The song is also her second highest charting song on <a href="/wiki/Adult_Top_40" title="Adult Top 40">Adult Pop Songs</a> behind "<a href="/wiki/Before_He_Cheats" title="Before He Cheats">Before He Cheats</a>", peaking at 20.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_popular_culture">In popular culture</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Good_Girl_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: In popular culture">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Good Girl" was made available for download on June 26, 2012 to play in <i><a href="/wiki/Rock_Band_3" title="Rock Band 3">Rock Band 3</a></i> Basic and Pro mode utilizing real guitar / bass guitar, and MIDI compatible electronic drum kits / keyboards plus vocal harmonies. The song is also available on the game <i><a href="/wiki/Just_Dance_4" title="Just Dance 4">Just Dance 4</a></i> available on the <a href="/wiki/Xbox_360" title="Xbox 360">Xbox 360</a> <a href="/wiki/Kinect" title="Kinect">Kinect</a>, <a href="/wiki/PlayStation_3" title="PlayStation 3">PlayStation 3</a> <a href="/wiki/PlayStation_Move" title="PlayStation Move">PlayStation Move</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wii" title="Wii">Wii</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wii_U" title="Wii U">Wii U</a>. The song is available as an exclusive to the NTSC version of the game in the Americas. The song is also a downloadable content (DLC) song on the European, Australian and New Zealand stores (PAL). This song is also available in <a href="/wiki/Guitar_Hero_Live" title="Guitar Hero Live">Guitar Hero Live</a>.
</p><p>Adriana Louise sang the song in the second week of The Live Rounds on the <a href="/wiki/The_Voice_(U.S._season_3)" class="mw-redirect" title="The Voice (U.S. season 3)">third season</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Voice_(U.S._TV_series)" class="mw-redirect" title="The Voice (U.S. TV series)">The Voice</a>.</i> Amber Carrington sang the song for her Blind Audition on the <a href="/wiki/The_Voice_(U.S._season_4)" class="mw-redirect" title="The Voice (U.S. season 4)">fourth season</a> of <i>The Voice.</i> Kat Perkins sang the song for her Instant Save on the <a href="/wiki/The_Voice_(U.S._season_6)" class="mw-redirect" title="The Voice (U.S. season 6)">sixth season</a> of <i>The Voice</i>; she later recorded a studio version for her 2014 EP, <i>Fearless</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
