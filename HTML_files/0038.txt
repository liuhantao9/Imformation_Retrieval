<p>The <b>51st Annual Grammy Awards</b> took place at the <a href="/wiki/Staples_Center" title="Staples Center">Staples Center</a> in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, on February 8, 2009. <a href="/wiki/Robert_Plant" title="Robert Plant">Robert Plant</a> &amp; <a href="/wiki/Alison_Krauss" title="Alison Krauss">Alison Krauss</a> were the biggest winners of the night, winning five awards including Album of the Year for their critically acclaimed album <i><a href="/wiki/Raising_Sand" title="Raising Sand">Raising Sand</a></i>. Krauss became the sixth female solo artist to have won 5 awards in one night joining <a href="/wiki/Lauryn_Hill" title="Lauryn Hill">Lauryn Hill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Keys" title="Alicia Keys">Alicia Keys</a>, <a href="/wiki/Norah_Jones" title="Norah Jones">Norah Jones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Beyonc%C3%A9" title="Beyoncé">Beyoncé Knowles</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Amy_Winehouse" title="Amy Winehouse">Amy Winehouse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-billboard1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-billboard1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lil_Wayne" title="Lil Wayne">Lil Wayne</a> received the most nominations with eight.
</p><p>The awards broadcast won a 2009 <a href="/wiki/Creative_Arts_Emmy" class="mw-redirect" title="Creative Arts Emmy">Creative Arts Emmy</a> for Outstanding Sound Mixing for a Variety or Music Series or Special.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Cancelled_performances">Cancelled performances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=51st_Annual_Grammy_Awards&amp;action=edit&amp;section=37" title="Edit section: Cancelled performances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Rihanna" title="Rihanna">Rihanna</a>'s 51st Grammy Awards performance was cancelled due to treatment caused by domestic violence by her then-boyfriend, <a href="/wiki/Chris_Brown" title="Chris Brown">Chris Brown</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Chris_Brown" title="Chris Brown">Chris Brown</a> had been arrested the same day as the event took place due to the assault on his then-girlfriend <a href="/wiki/Rihanna" title="Rihanna">Rihanna</a>.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Records">Records</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=51st_Annual_Grammy_Awards&amp;action=edit&amp;section=38" title="Edit section: Records">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Alison_Krauss" title="Alison Krauss">Alison Krauss</a> became the sixth female solo artist to have won five Grammys in one evening. The artists who won five before her are <a href="/wiki/Lauryn_Hill" title="Lauryn Hill">Lauryn Hill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alicia_Keys" title="Alicia Keys">Alicia Keys</a>, <a href="/wiki/Norah_Jones" title="Norah Jones">Norah Jones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Beyonc%C3%A9_Knowles" class="mw-redirect" title="Beyoncé Knowles">Beyoncé Knowles</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Amy_Winehouse" title="Amy Winehouse">Amy Winehouse</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-billboard1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-billboard1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, at the ceremony (<a href="/wiki/2010_Grammy_Awards" class="mw-redirect" title="2010 Grammy Awards">2010 Grammy Awards</a>) next year, Beyoncé surpassed the record by gaining six such awards.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=51st_Annual_Grammy_Awards&amp;action=edit&amp;section=40" title="Edit section: Trivia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
