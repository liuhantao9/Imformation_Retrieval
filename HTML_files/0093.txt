<p>"<b>Just Stand Up!</b>" is a song performed by an all-star <a href="/wiki/Charity_supergroup" title="Charity supergroup">charity supergroup</a> of <a href="/wiki/Pop_music" title="Pop music">pop</a>, <a href="/wiki/Contemporary_R%26B" title="Contemporary R&amp;B">R&amp;B</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Country_music" title="Country music">country</a> artists during the telethon "<a href="/wiki/Stand_Up_to_Cancer" title="Stand Up to Cancer">Stand Up to Cancer</a>". The group was purposely billed as <a href="#Performers_(in_order_of_appearance)">Artists Stand Up to Cancer</a> to the public.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Just_Stand_Up!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>American songwriter and producer <a href="/wiki/L.A._Reid" title="L.A. Reid">Antonio "L.A." Reid</a> conceived of the collaboration after a meeting with the founder of Stand Up to Cancer, which benefited from the proceeds of the record. Reid co-produced the track with American singer <a href="/wiki/Kenneth_%22Babyface%22_Edmonds" class="mw-redirect" title="Kenneth &quot;Babyface&quot; Edmonds">Kenneth "Babyface" Edmonds</a>, and the single was released on August 21, 2008.
</p><p>The singers performed the song live on the "Stand Up to Cancer" special, which aired simultaneously on US TV networks <a href="/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company" title="American Broadcasting Company">ABC</a>, <a href="/wiki/CBS" title="CBS">CBS</a> and <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> on September 5, 2008.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The single was re-released on September 30, 2008, with the original recording, as well as the audio of the live performance. The CD was enhanced so it had special features for a computer. A <a href="/wiki/Music_video" title="Music video">music video</a> was not produced for the single.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Chart_performance">Chart performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Just_Stand_Up!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Chart performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The song debuted on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a> at 78 on the week of September 13, 2008. The next week it was the greatest sales gainer and jumped 67 places to land at number 11. The next week, however, the song fell to number 36. It also peaked on the <i>Billboard</i> <a href="/wiki/Pop_100" title="Pop 100">Pop 100</a> chart at 18. As of October 2013, "Just Stand Up!" had sold 393,000 digital downloads in the US.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> On the <a href="/wiki/UK_Singles_Chart" title="UK Singles Chart">UK Singles Chart</a>, it debuted at number 39, before climbing the chart to number 26.<sup id="cite_ref-uk_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-uk-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> On the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Hot_100" title="Canadian Hot 100">Canadian Hot 100</a> chart it made a "Hot Shot Debut" at number 10 in the week of September 11, 2008, based on downloads.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Live_performance">Live performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Just_Stand_Up!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Live performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The live performance was held on the "Stand Up 2 Cancer" event on September 5, 2008, aired by multiple channels. All the original artists appeared for the performance, except <a href="/wiki/LeAnn_Rimes" title="LeAnn Rimes">LeAnn Rimes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sheryl_Crow" title="Sheryl Crow">Sheryl Crow</a> and <a href="/wiki/Melissa_Etheridge" title="Melissa Etheridge">Melissa Etheridge</a>, with the addition of <a href="/wiki/Nicole_Scherzinger" title="Nicole Scherzinger">Nicole Scherzinger</a> from <a href="/wiki/The_Pussycat_Dolls" title="The Pussycat Dolls">The Pussycat Dolls</a> singing the parts of Sheryl Crow. The live performance is slightly different from the original song available for download. The live performance video is available for purchase on <a href="/wiki/ITunes" title="ITunes">iTunes</a>.
</p>
