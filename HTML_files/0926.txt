<p>The county of <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a> is divided into five Metropolitan Boroughs. The metropolitan boroughs of <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a> are <a href="/wiki/Leeds" title="Leeds">Leeds</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wakefield" title="Wakefield">Wakefield</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a>, <a href="/wiki/Calderdale" title="Calderdale">Calderdale</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bradford" title="Bradford">Bradford</a>.
</p><p>As there are 413 Grade II* listed buildings in the county they have been split into separate lists for each borough.
