<p><b>Albina</b> is a town in eastern <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>, and is capital of the <a href="/wiki/Marowijne_District" title="Marowijne District">Marowijne District</a>. The town lies on the west bank of the <a href="/wiki/Maroni_River" class="mw-redirect" title="Maroni River">Marowijne river</a> (Maroni river), which forms the border with <a href="/wiki/French_Guiana" title="French Guiana">French Guiana</a>, directly opposite the French Guianan town of <a href="/wiki/Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni" title="Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni">Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni</a>, to which it is connected by a frequent <a href="/wiki/Ferry" title="Ferry">ferry</a> service.
</p><p>Albina can be reached by bus via the <a href="/wiki/East-West_Link_(Suriname)" title="East-West Link (Suriname)">East-West Link</a>. The distance between Paramaribo and Albina is about 150 kilometres (95&#160;mi). Because of the war in the second half of the 1980s a great part of the area including the road to Albina was destroyed. Much of Albina was destroyed during the <a href="/wiki/Maroon_(people)" title="Maroon (people)">Maroon</a> rebellions in the 1980s–1990s.
</p><p>The town has a small airport, the <a href="/wiki/Albina_Airstrip" title="Albina Airstrip">Albina Airstrip</a>, with a 650-metre (2,150&#160;ft) asphalt runway in use since 1953.
</p><p>Albina has a small boat (korjaal) connection to <a href="/wiki/Galibi,_Suriname" title="Galibi, Suriname">Galibi</a>. The journey across the water takes about 1.5 hrs. There is a beach and a small tourist shop. The main reason tourists visit Galibi is to see the turtles, which come from all over the world (including places as distant as <a href="/wiki/Costa_Rica" title="Costa Rica">Costa Rica</a> and <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>) to lay their eggs in Suriname.  Poachers also come, to collect the eggs to sell them on the market.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2010)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
