	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en"><i>Times of Suriname</i></h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>

<p><i><b>Times of Suriname</b></i> is a national newspaper in <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-de_Vries_2008_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-de_Vries_2008-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The paper is published daily (except on Sundays) in a <a href="/wiki/Broadsheet" title="Broadsheet">broadsheet</a> format with a reported circulation of 35,000-40,000.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Foriers_2018_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Foriers_2018-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The paper was founded in December 2003 by (at the time) rich business man and politician <a href="/w/index.php?title=Rudi_Dilip_Sardjoe&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Rudi Dilip Sardjoe (page does not exist)">Rudi Dilip Sardjoe</a> and claims to be a quality news paper and the largest and most-read paper of the country.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Content">Content</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Times_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Content">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <i>Times of Suriname</i> is one of the main newspapers of the country,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and publishes news articles primarily in <a href="/wiki/Dutch_language" title="Dutch language">Dutch</a>, while also publishing some articles in <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a> (in 2005 it experimented with some articles in <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_language" title="Portuguese language">Portuguese</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-Gobardhan-Rambocus_2013_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Gobardhan-Rambocus_2013-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The paper contains news on Suriname, the Dutch Caribbean and the Netherlands.<sup id="cite_ref-Gobardhan-Rambocus_2013_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Gobardhan-Rambocus_2013-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The paper is reported to have some 20 journalists under employment, most of them young.<sup id="cite_ref-de_Vries_2008_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-de_Vries_2008-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Foriers_2018_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Foriers_2018-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
