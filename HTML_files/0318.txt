<p><b>Para</b> is a district of northern <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>. Para's <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital city</a> is <a href="/wiki/Onverwacht,_Suriname" title="Onverwacht, Suriname">Onverwacht</a>, with other towns including <a href="/wiki/Paranam" title="Paranam">Paranam</a>, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sabana,_Para,_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Sabana, Para, Suriname (page does not exist)">Sabana</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zanderij" title="Zanderij">Zanderij</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="The_district">The district</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Para_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: The district">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Para has a <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 24,700 and an <a href="/wiki/Area" title="Area">area</a> of 5,393&#160;km².<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The district is the <a href="/wiki/Mining" title="Mining">mining</a> and <a href="/wiki/Forestry" title="Forestry">forestry</a> centre of Suriname, with many large <a href="/wiki/Bauxite" title="Bauxite">bauxite</a> mining operations operating.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2009)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> The ruins of the city of <a href="/wiki/Jodensavanne" title="Jodensavanne">Jodensavanne</a> are in Para district. <a href="/wiki/Jew" class="mw-redirect" title="Jew">Jews</a> fleeing the <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Inquisition" title="Spanish Inquisition">Spanish Inquisition</a> established Jodensavanne in the 17th century, but it was destroyed in 1832 by a <a href="/wiki/Fire" title="Fire">fire</a>. Jodensavanne was an <a href="/wiki/Internment_camp" class="mw-redirect" title="Internment camp">internment camp</a> for suspected <a href="/wiki/Nazism" title="Nazism">Nazi</a> supporters in Suriname during the <a href="/wiki/Second_World_War" class="mw-redirect" title="Second World War">Second World War</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2009)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> Para is also home to a <a href="/wiki/Spring_(hydrosphere)" class="mw-redirect" title="Spring (hydrosphere)">spring</a> that supposedly has <a href="/wiki/Medicinal_properties" class="mw-redirect" title="Medicinal properties">medicinal properties</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2009)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Para_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
