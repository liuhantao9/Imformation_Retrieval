<p><b>Nickerie</b> is a district of <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>, on the north-west <a href="/wiki/Coast" title="Coast">coast</a>. Nickerie's <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital city</a> is <a href="/wiki/Nieuw-Nickerie" class="mw-redirect" title="Nieuw-Nickerie">Nieuw-Nickerie</a>, the second largest <a href="/wiki/City" title="City">city</a> in the country. Other towns include <a href="/w/index.php?title=Washabo&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Washabo (page does not exist)">Washabo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wageningen,_Suriname" title="Wageningen, Suriname">Wageningen</a>. The district borders the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a> to the north, the Surinamese district of <a href="/wiki/Coronie" class="mw-redirect" title="Coronie">Coronie</a> to the east, the Surinamese district of <a href="/wiki/Sipaliwini" title="Sipaliwini">Sipaliwini</a> to the south and <a href="/wiki/Guyana" title="Guyana">Guyana</a> to the west.
</p><p>Nickerie has a <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 36.639 (2004) and an <a href="/wiki/Area" title="Area">area</a> of 5,353&#160;km².
</p><p>Relations between Suriname and neighbouring Guyana have always been tense, and border disputes in the south of the country (with occasional sporadic fighting) mean that ports of entry to Guyana from Suriname are few. However, there is a <a href="/wiki/Ferry" title="Ferry">ferry</a> that sails between Molson Creek in Guyana and the district of Nickerie. Nickerie's population includes <a href="/wiki/Indo-Surinamese" title="Indo-Surinamese">East Indian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Javanese_people" title="Javanese people">Javanese</a>, <a href="/wiki/Afro-Surinamese" title="Afro-Surinamese">Afro-Surinamese</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chinese_people" title="Chinese people">Chinese</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_people" title="Portuguese people">Portuguese people</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Banana" title="Banana">Bananas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rice" title="Rice">rice</a> are the main crops grown in Nickerie.
</p>
