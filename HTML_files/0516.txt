<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Railways">Railways</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Transport_in_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Railways">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Rail_transport" title="Rail transport">Railways</a>, total: 166&#160;km <a href="/wiki/Single_track_(rail)" class="mw-redirect" title="Single track (rail)">single track</a>.
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Standard_gauge" class="mw-redirect" title="Standard gauge">standard gauge</a>: 80&#160;km <span class="nowrap">1,435&#160;mm</span> (<span class="nowrap">4&#160;ft&#160;<span class="frac">8<span class="visualhide">&#160;</span><sup>1</sup>&#8260;<sub>2</sub></span>&#160;in</span>) gauge in West-Suriname, but not in use. This stretch was constructed as part of the <a href="/wiki/West_Suriname_Plan" title="West Suriname Plan">West Suriname Plan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Narrow_gauge_railway" class="mw-redirect" title="Narrow gauge railway">narrow gauge</a>: 86&#160;km <span class="nowrap">1,000&#160;mm</span> (<span class="nowrap">3&#160;ft&#160;<span class="frac">3<span class="visualhide">&#160;</span><sup>3</sup>&#8260;<sub>8</sub></span>&#160;in</span>) gauge <a href="/wiki/Lawa_Railway" title="Lawa Railway">Lawa Railway</a> from Onverdacht to Brownsberg, currently not in use but a plan has been announced to reopen the line between Onverwacht and Paramaribo Central Station. The completion date for phase one was hoped to be 2016 and the intention is for the line to be extended onto Paramaribo Adolf Pengel Airport.</li></ul></li></ul>
