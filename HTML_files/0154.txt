<p><b>Indigenous peoples in Suriname</b>,  <b>Native Surinamese</b>, or <b>Amerindian Surinamese</b>, are <a href="/wiki/Surinamese_people" title="Surinamese people">Surinamese people</a> who are of <a href="/wiki/Indigenous_peoples_in_South_America" class="mw-redirect" title="Indigenous peoples in South America">indigenous</a> ancestry. They comprise approximately 3.8% of <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>'s population of 566,846.<sup id="cite_ref-cia_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
