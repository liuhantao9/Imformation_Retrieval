<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Salterhebble_-_geograph.org.uk_-_391253.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d7/Salterhebble_-_geograph.org.uk_-_391253.jpg/220px-Salterhebble_-_geograph.org.uk_-_391253.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="167" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d7/Salterhebble_-_geograph.org.uk_-_391253.jpg/330px-Salterhebble_-_geograph.org.uk_-_391253.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d7/Salterhebble_-_geograph.org.uk_-_391253.jpg/440px-Salterhebble_-_geograph.org.uk_-_391253.jpg 2x" data-file-width="640" data-file-height="486" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Salterhebble_-_geograph.org.uk_-_391253.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Salterhebble, West Yorkshire</div></div></div>
<p><b>Salterhebble</b> is an area of <a href="/wiki/Halifax,_West_Yorkshire" title="Halifax, West Yorkshire">Halifax</a>, a town within the <a href="/wiki/Calderdale" title="Calderdale">Metropolitan Borough of Calderdale</a>, in <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. The town falls within the Skircoat ward of Calderdale Council.  Salterhebble is located where the <a href="/wiki/Hebble_Brook" title="Hebble Brook">Hebble Brook</a> flows into the <a href="/wiki/River_Calder,_West_Yorkshire" title="River Calder, West Yorkshire">River Calder</a>. It is on the main route into Halifax from <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> and <a href="/wiki/Brighouse" title="Brighouse">Brighouse</a>. Salterhebble is the home of Calderdale Royal Hospital, a school, a fast food restaurant, a funeral parlour (formerly The Falcon pub) and one active pub, The Watermill. Some former pubs in the village included The Punch Bowl, The Falcon and The Stafford Arms.
</p><p>The place-name 'Salterhebble' means "the salt-sellers' foot-bridge".<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Hospital">Hospital</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Salterhebble&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Hospital">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:The_Calderdale_Royal_Hospital_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1012757.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2f/The_Calderdale_Royal_Hospital_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1012757.jpg/220px-The_Calderdale_Royal_Hospital_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1012757.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2f/The_Calderdale_Royal_Hospital_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1012757.jpg/330px-The_Calderdale_Royal_Hospital_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1012757.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2f/The_Calderdale_Royal_Hospital_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1012757.jpg/440px-The_Calderdale_Royal_Hospital_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1012757.jpg 2x" data-file-width="640" data-file-height="480" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:The_Calderdale_Royal_Hospital_-_geograph.org.uk_-_1012757.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Calderdale Royal Hospital, Halifax</div></div></div>
<p><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.cht.nhs.uk/about-us/our-hospitals/calderdale-royal-hospital/">Calderdale Royal Hospital</a> (part of Calderdale &amp; Huddersfield <a href="/wiki/NHS_Foundation_Trust" class="mw-redirect" title="NHS Foundation Trust">NHS Foundation Trust</a>) is located in Salterhebble. It has specialist departments and Calderdale's <a href="/wiki/Accident_and_Emergency" class="mw-redirect" title="Accident and Emergency">A&amp;E</a> department and the Calderdale Birth Centre.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The hospital was built and opened in 2001, on the site of the original Halifax General Hospital, following the merger with the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Halifax_Infirmary" title="Royal Halifax Infirmary">Royal Halifax Infirmary</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Canal">Canal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Salterhebble&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Canal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Salterhebble_Lock_Keeper%27s_Cottage_-_Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation_-_geograph.org.uk_-_714506.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/39/Salterhebble_Lock_Keeper%27s_Cottage_-_Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation_-_geograph.org.uk_-_714506.jpg/220px-Salterhebble_Lock_Keeper%27s_Cottage_-_Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation_-_geograph.org.uk_-_714506.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/39/Salterhebble_Lock_Keeper%27s_Cottage_-_Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation_-_geograph.org.uk_-_714506.jpg/330px-Salterhebble_Lock_Keeper%27s_Cottage_-_Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation_-_geograph.org.uk_-_714506.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/39/Salterhebble_Lock_Keeper%27s_Cottage_-_Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation_-_geograph.org.uk_-_714506.jpg/440px-Salterhebble_Lock_Keeper%27s_Cottage_-_Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation_-_geograph.org.uk_-_714506.jpg 2x" data-file-width="640" data-file-height="480" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Salterhebble_Lock_Keeper%27s_Cottage_-_Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation_-_geograph.org.uk_-_714506.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Salterhebble Lock Keeper's Cottage</div></div></div>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Calder_and_Hebble_Navigation" title="Calder and Hebble Navigation">Calder and Hebble Navigation</a> has a short branch at Salterhebble. This is officially called the Halifax Arm (because it used to lead into the town centre), but is usually called the Salterhebble Arm.  The branch terminates at Salterhebble Basin. Next to the point where the branch leaves the main line is the triple flight of Salterhebble Locks, one of which is a candidate for the shortest lock on the connected inland waterway system of Britain.
</p>
