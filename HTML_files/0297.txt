	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">CARIFORUM</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">This article is about the international organisation. For the Malaysian internet forum, see <a href="/wiki/Cari_Internet" title="Cari Internet">Cari Internet</a>.</div>
<p>The <b>Caribbean Forum</b> (<b>CARIFORUM</b>) is a subgroup of the <a href="/wiki/African,_Caribbean_and_Pacific_Group_of_States" title="African, Caribbean and Pacific Group of States">African, Caribbean and Pacific Group of States</a> and serves as a base for economic dialogue with the <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European Union</a>. It was established in 1992. Its membership comprises the 15 <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Community" title="Caribbean Community">Caribbean Community</a> states, along with the <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Republic" title="Dominican Republic">Dominican Republic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Caribbean360_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Caribbean360-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In 2008, they signed an <a href="/wiki/Economic_Partnership_Agreements" title="Economic Partnership Agreements">Economic Partnership Agreement</a> with the <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European Union</a>, though <a href="/wiki/Guyana" title="Guyana">Guyana</a> and <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">Haiti</a> had expressed reservations and did not attend the signing ceremony.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Tensions within the group have grown over issues of trade and immigration; the Dominican Republic, with the group's largest economy, has expressed reservations over its current structure.<sup id="cite_ref-Caribbean360_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Caribbean360-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
