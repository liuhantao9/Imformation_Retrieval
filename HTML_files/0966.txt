		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Motor Torpedo Boat Squadrons Training Center</b> is a former <a href="/wiki/United_States_Navy" title="United States Navy">United States Navy</a> training facility in <a href="/wiki/Melville,_Rhode_Island" title="Melville, Rhode Island">Melville, Rhode Island</a>, which instructed naval personnel in the operation of <a href="/wiki/PT_boat" title="PT boat">PT boats</a>. It was closed after <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a> ended.
</p>
