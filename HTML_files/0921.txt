	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">West Yorkshire Joint Services</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>West Yorkshire Joint Services</b> (<b>WYJS</b>) provides a range of public services to the five districts of <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> (<a href="/wiki/City_of_Bradford" title="City of Bradford">Bradford</a>, <a href="/wiki/Calderdale" title="Calderdale">Calderdale</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a>, <a href="/wiki/City_of_Leeds" title="City of Leeds">Leeds</a> and <a href="/wiki/City_of_Wakefield" title="City of Wakefield">Wakefield</a>). It is jointly funded by the five district councils, pro rata to their population, and is run by a committee of equal numbers of councillors from the five councils.  It "brings together a number of key services which the five district councils of West Yorkshire recognise can be more effectively delivered on a county-wide basis".<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire_Metropolitan_County_Council" class="mw-redirect" title="West Yorkshire Metropolitan County Council">West Yorkshire Metropolitan County Council</a> was abolished in 1988, a "Joint Committee Arrangement" was established, to continue to provide archaeology, archives and trading standards services for the five successor district councils. In 1997 the Joint Services were created, to avoid duplication of resources across these services; it is based near <a href="/wiki/Morley,_West_Yorkshire" title="Morley, West Yorkshire">Morley</a>, Leeds. 
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services.jpg/220px-West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="87" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services.jpg/330px-West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services.jpg/440px-West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4603" data-file-height="1816" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>West Yorkshire Joint Services Building in Morley</div></div></div>
<p>Joint Services comprises:
</p>
<ul><li><b>West Yorkshire Archaeology Advisory Service</b></li>
<li><b>Archaeological Services WYAS</b>, a commercial organisation</li>
<li><b>West Yorkshire Archive Service</b></li>
<li><b>West Yorkshire Ecology</b></li>
<li><b>West Yorkshire Materials Testing Service</b></li>
<li><b>West Yorkshire Analytical Services</b></li>
<li><b>West Yorkshire <a href="/wiki/Trading_Standards" title="Trading Standards">Trading Standards</a></b></li>
<li><b>West Yorkshire Calibration Services</b></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Archive_Service">Archive Service</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=West_Yorkshire_Joint_Services&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Archive Service">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The archive service operates at five locations: in <a href="/wiki/Wakefield" title="Wakefield">Wakefield</a> (incorporating the West Riding Registry of Deeds); <a href="/wiki/Bradford" title="Bradford">Bradford</a> Central Library, <a href="/wiki/Halifax,_West_Yorkshire" title="Halifax, West Yorkshire">Halifax</a> Central Library in Calderdale, <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> Central Library in Kirklees, and the former <a href="/wiki/Sheepscar" title="Sheepscar">Sheepscar</a> branch library building near the centre of Leeds.
</p>
