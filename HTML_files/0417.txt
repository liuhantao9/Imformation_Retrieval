<p><b>Nieuw Nickerie</b><sup id="cite_ref-GEOnet_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GEOnet-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> (pronounced 'Niekérie') is the third largest city in <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a> with a population estimated at 12,818. It is the <a href="/wiki/Capital_(political)" class="mw-redirect" title="Capital (political)">capital city</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Nickerie_District" title="Nickerie District">Nickerie</a> <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Suriname" title="Districts of Suriname">district</a>, and the terminus of the <a href="/wiki/East-West_Link_(Suriname)" title="East-West Link (Suriname)">East-West Link</a>.
</p><p>Nieuw Nickerie lies on the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/Nickerie_(river)" class="mw-redirect" title="Nickerie (river)">Nickerie river</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic" class="mw-redirect" title="Atlantic">Atlantic</a> coast, opposite the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/Courantyne_River" title="Courantyne River">Corantijn river</a> (Courantyne) and the Guyanese town of <a href="/wiki/Corriverton" title="Corriverton">Corriverton</a> (Springlands), to which a <a href="/wiki/Ferry" title="Ferry">ferry</a> service operates.
</p><p>The main industries are <a href="/wiki/Banana" title="Banana">bananas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rice" title="Rice">rice</a>. The town contains a market and several hotels, including the Hotel Ameerali, Hotel de President, Hotel Tropical, Hotel de Vesting and Residence Inn. The only Surinamese hospital outside of Paramaribo, <a href="/wiki/Lachmipersad_Mungra_Regional_Hospital_Nickerie" title="Lachmipersad Mungra Regional Hospital Nickerie">Lachmipersad Mungra Regional Hospital Nickerie</a>, is located on the Annastraat in Nieuw-Nickerie.
</p><p>Nieuw Nickerie was built in 1879 after the former center of the district, <a href="/wiki/Nieuw_Rotterdam" title="Nieuw Rotterdam">Nieuw Rotterdam</a>, was destroyed by floods.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Majoor_Henry_Fernandes_Airport" title="Majoor Henry Fernandes Airport">Majoor Henry Fernandes Airport</a>, also known as <i>Nieuw Nickerie Airport</i>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> is located near the city.
</p>
