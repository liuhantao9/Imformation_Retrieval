<p>The <b>Nickerie</b> is a river in the northwestern part of <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>.
</p><p>The river originates in the <a href="/wiki/Bakhuis_Mountains" title="Bakhuis Mountains">Bakhuis Mountains</a> and then flows to the North where it forms part of the border between the <a href="/wiki/Coronie" class="mw-redirect" title="Coronie">Coronie</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nickerie_District" title="Nickerie District">Nickerie</a> districts. The river then turns West and flows into the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a> via <a href="/wiki/Wageningen_(Suriname)" class="mw-redirect" title="Wageningen (Suriname)">Wageningen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nieuw-Nickerie" class="mw-redirect" title="Nieuw-Nickerie">Nieuw-Nickerie</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Blanche_Marie_Falls" title="Blanche Marie Falls">Blanche Marie Falls</a> are located on the river.
</p><p>There is a bridge near <a href="/wiki/Groot_Henar" title="Groot Henar">Groot Henar</a> that is part of the Northern <a href="/wiki/East-West_Link_(Suriname)" title="East-West Link (Suriname)">East-West Link</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Another, more basic <a href="/wiki/Bailey_bridge" title="Bailey bridge">bailey bridge</a> was built near <a href="/w/index.php?title=Kamp_52&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Kamp 52 (page does not exist)">Kamp 52</a> on the Southern East-West Link.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
