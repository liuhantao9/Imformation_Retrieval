<p>The <b>Huddersfield Royal Infirmary</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Hospital" title="Hospital">hospital</a> situated in the <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> town of <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, part of <a href="/wiki/Calderdale_and_Huddersfield_NHS_Foundation_Trust" title="Calderdale and Huddersfield NHS Foundation Trust">Calderdale and Huddersfield NHS Foundation Trust</a>. It is situated in the suburb of <a href="/wiki/Lindley,_Huddersfield" title="Lindley, Huddersfield">Lindley</a> and provides general services, emergency services and some specialist services.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Huddersfield_Royal_Infirmary&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:The_Old_Royal_Infirmary_-_geograph.org.uk_-_359164.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f9/The_Old_Royal_Infirmary_-_geograph.org.uk_-_359164.jpg/220px-The_Old_Royal_Infirmary_-_geograph.org.uk_-_359164.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f9/The_Old_Royal_Infirmary_-_geograph.org.uk_-_359164.jpg/330px-The_Old_Royal_Infirmary_-_geograph.org.uk_-_359164.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f9/The_Old_Royal_Infirmary_-_geograph.org.uk_-_359164.jpg/440px-The_Old_Royal_Infirmary_-_geograph.org.uk_-_359164.jpg 2x" data-file-width="640" data-file-height="480" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:The_Old_Royal_Infirmary_-_geograph.org.uk_-_359164.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The old Infirmary in New North Road</div></div></div>
<p>The original hospital in <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a>, which was designed by <a href="/wiki/Joseph_Kay_(architect)" title="Joseph Kay (architect)">Joseph Kay</a> and which was located at New North Road, opened in 1831.<sup id="cite_ref-he_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-he-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Additions included the south wing in 1861, the north wing in 1874 and the Carlisle Wing, financed by a gift from <a href="/wiki/Hildred_Carlile" title="Hildred Carlile">Sir Hildred Carlile</a>, in 1898.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The foundation stone for a further extension was laid by the <a href="/wiki/George_VI" title="George VI">Duke of York</a> in 1932; it was opened by <a href="/wiki/Berkeley_Moynihan,_1st_Baron_Moynihan" title="Berkeley Moynihan, 1st Baron Moynihan">Lord Moynihan</a> in 1934.<sup id="cite_ref-he_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-he-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The infirmary moved to new facilities at Lindley which were completed in 1965 and formally opened by <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="Prime Minister of the United Kingdom">Prime Minister</a> <a href="/wiki/Harold_Wilson" title="Harold Wilson">Harold Wilson</a> in January 1967.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It became the base for Huddersfield Pharmacy Specials, a business which had been established several years earlier and which produces mainly unlicensed medicines and bespoke creams and ointments, oral liquids, eye drops and injectables.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In April 2006, <a href="/wiki/Photovoltaic_array" class="mw-redirect" title="Photovoltaic array">photovoltaic array</a> panels were installed on the roof of the hospital building, providing it with a <a href="/wiki/Renewable_source" class="mw-redirect" title="Renewable source">renewable source</a> of <a href="/wiki/Electricity" title="Electricity">electricity</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
