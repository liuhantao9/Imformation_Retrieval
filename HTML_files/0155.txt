It is the ninth-most-common <a href="/wiki/Surname" title="Surname">surname</a> in <a href="/wiki/Denmark" title="Denmark">Denmark</a>, shared by about 1.9% of the population.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
