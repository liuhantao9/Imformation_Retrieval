<p><b>Anton de Kom University</b> (<a href="/wiki/Dutch_language" title="Dutch language">Dutch</a>: <i lang="nl"><b>Anton de Kom Universiteit van Suriname</b></i>) is the only university in Suriname. It is located in the capital, <a href="/wiki/Paramaribo" title="Paramaribo">Paramaribo</a>, and named for <a href="/wiki/Anton_de_Kom" title="Anton de Kom">Anton de Kom</a>, an anti-colonialist activist who was killed by the <a href="/wiki/Nazi_Party" title="Nazi Party">Nazis</a> while in exile in the <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anton_de_Kom_University_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Founding">Founding</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anton_de_Kom_University_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Founding">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tertiary education in Suriname dates back to the 19th century. In 1882 there was already tertiary education provided at the "Geneeskundige School" and there also existed an organized juridical education (The Law-school), which was founded in the late forties. Furthermore, there were other para-university courses, namely the discipline to become a <a href="/wiki/Surveying" title="Surveying">surveyor</a>, dentist and <a href="/wiki/Pharmacist" title="Pharmacist">pharmacist</a>.
</p><p>At the centenary of the "Staten of Suriname" in 1966, this organization took the important decision to cooperate with the government of Suriname to found a university.  The proclamation was made on November 1, 1968, in the then still existing Theatre Star. Since then, the first of November is celebrated as Founders' Day.
</p><p>On November 1, 1968, the Faculty of Law was started and other faculties followed:
</p>
<ul><li>The Faculty der Medische Wetenschappen on September, 26th  1969</li>
<li>The Faculty der Sociaal – Economische Wetenschappen on November, 1st 1975</li>
<li>The Faculty der Natuurtechnische Wetenschappen on December, 1st 1976</li>
<li>The Faculty der Technische Wetenschappen on December, 1st 1977</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Faculties">Faculties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anton_de_Kom_University_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Faculties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Anton de Kom University of Suriname has the following faculties:<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li>The Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Healthcare_science" class="mw-redirect" title="Healthcare science">Medical Sciences</a>
<ul><li>Medicine and <a href="/wiki/Physiotherapy" class="mw-redirect" title="Physiotherapy">Physiotherapy</a></li></ul></li>
<li>The Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Social_sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Social sciences">Social Sciences</a>
<ul><li>Law</li>
<li>Economics, main subjects General Economics and Business Economics</li>
<li>Management of Education and the Study of Social Changes within Society</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Public_Administration" class="mw-redirect" title="Public Administration">Public Administration</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sociology" title="Sociology">Sociology</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Business_Management" class="mw-redirect" title="Business Management">Business Management</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Psychology" title="Psychology">Psychology</a></li></ul></li>
<li>The Faculty of Technological Sciences
<ul><li>Agricultural Production, main subjects <a href="/wiki/Soil_science" title="Soil science">Soil Sciences</a>, <a href="/wiki/Forestry" title="Forestry">Forestry</a>, Agriculture, <a href="/wiki/Animal_Husbandry" class="mw-redirect" title="Animal Husbandry">Animal Husbandry</a></li>
<li>Mineral Production, main subjects Geology, Mining</li>
<li>Electrical Engineering, main subjects Energy Technique, Information Technique</li>
<li>Infrastructure, main subjects Building and Construction, <a href="/wiki/Civil_engineering" title="Civil engineering">Civil Engineering</a>, <a href="/wiki/Geoinformatics" title="Geoinformatics">Geoinformatics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Land_management" title="Land management">Land</a> and <a href="/wiki/Water_management" class="mw-redirect" title="Water management">Water Management</a></li>
<li>Environmental Sciences, main subjects Management of Aquatic Resources, Management of Environmental and Natural Resources</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mechanical_engineering" title="Mechanical engineering">Mechanical Engineering</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> main subjects Production Technology, Engineering Mechanics and Materials, Process and Energy</li></ul></li>
<li>The Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Humanities" title="Humanities">Humanities</a>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Dutch_language" title="Dutch language">Dutch</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/History" title="History">History</a></li></ul></li>
<li>The Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Mathematical_sciences" title="Mathematical sciences">Mathematical</a> and <a href="/wiki/Outline_of_physical_science" title="Outline of physical science">Physical</a> Sciences
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Chemistry" title="Chemistry">Chemistry</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mathematics" title="Mathematics">Mathematics</a></li></ul></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Board">Board</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anton_de_Kom_University_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Board">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The University Board is the highest governing body of the University and has the entire responsibility of the organization. Prof. dr. Jack Menke is the current president.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Research_institutes">Research institutes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anton_de_Kom_University_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Research institutes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There are five research centers doing research within their specific fields, and also rendering services to the community. The following are research centers: 
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Centre_for_Agricultural_Research_in_Suriname" title="Centre for Agricultural Research in Suriname">Centre for Agricultural Research in Suriname</a> (CELOS)</li></ul>
<dl><dd>Objective: promoting agricultural scientific education and research at the Faculty of Technological Sciences</dd></dl>
<ul><li>Institute for Applied Technology (INTEC)</li></ul>
<dl><dd>Objective: executing research projects in the field of technology</dd></dl>
<ul><li>Bio-Medical Research Institute Prof. dr. Paul Flu (MWI)</li></ul>
<dl><dd>Objective: promoting scientific education and research at the Faculty of Medical Sciences</dd></dl>
<ul><li>Institute for Development Planning and Management (IDPM)</li></ul>
<dl><dd>Objective: supporting the development policy of the Surinamese government</dd></dl>
<ul><li>Institute for Social Science Research (IMWO)</li></ul>
<dl><dd>Objective: executing social scientific research and rendering service</dd></dl>
