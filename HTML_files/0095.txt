<p>"<b>Can't Stop Lovin' You</b>" is a <a href="/wiki/Power_ballad" class="mw-redirect" title="Power ballad">power ballad</a> by American <a href="/wiki/Hard_rock" title="Hard rock">hard rock</a> band <a href="/wiki/Aerosmith" title="Aerosmith">Aerosmith</a> featuring American country recording artist <a href="/wiki/Carrie_Underwood" title="Carrie Underwood">Carrie Underwood</a> that was released on April 6, 2013,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> as the fourth single from the Aerosmith album <i><a href="/wiki/Music_from_Another_Dimension!" title="Music from Another Dimension!">Music from Another Dimension!</a></i>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Can%27t_Stop_Lovin%27_You_(Aerosmith_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Aerosmith lead singer and songwriter <a href="/wiki/Steven_Tyler" title="Steven Tyler">Steven Tyler</a> and frequent collaborator <a href="/wiki/Marti_Frederiksen" title="Marti Frederiksen">Marti Frederiksen</a> wrote the song with the band during sessions for <i>Music from Another Dimension!</i>. Tyler sang it in an "emotional drawl" similar to the band's 1993 hit "<a href="/wiki/Cryin%27" title="Cryin&#39;">Cryin'</a>" and the song took on a romantic, country sound. 
</p><p>Tyler commented, "When it was done, it was discussed that I might have sang it a little too country. And all along we thought, should we get someone in?" Tyler contacted country singer <a href="/wiki/Carrie_Underwood" title="Carrie Underwood">Carrie Underwood</a>, whom he had collaborated with in recent years, including a duet at a 2012 <a href="/wiki/Country_Music_Television" class="mw-redirect" title="Country Music Television">Country Music Television</a> Special and a performance at the 2011 <a href="/wiki/Academy_of_Country_Music_Awards" title="Academy of Country Music Awards">Academy of Country Music Awards</a>, where Tyler and Underwood performed the Aerosmith classic "<a href="/wiki/Walk_This_Way" title="Walk This Way">Walk This Way</a>" together, as well as Underwood's song "<a href="/wiki/Undo_It" title="Undo It">Undo It</a>", which was co-written by Frederiksen. The day Tyler contacted Underwood, she happened to be in town but had to leave the next morning, so Tyler asked her if she could come into record that night, and she agreed.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Response">Response</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Can%27t_Stop_Lovin%27_You_(Aerosmith_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Response">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>About two-and-a-half months after the release of <i>Music from Another Dimension!</i>, "Can't Stop Lovin' You" was released as a single to <a href="/wiki/Adult_contemporary_music" title="Adult contemporary music">adult contemporary</a>, <a href="/wiki/Adult_contemporary_music#Hot_adult_contemporary" title="Adult contemporary music">hot adult contemporary</a>, and modern radio formats on January 21, 2013.<sup id="cite_ref-All_Access_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-All_Access-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Examiner.com" title="Examiner.com">Examiner.com</a> wrote that the song features "country-pop guitar licks and an earworm melody right up Underwood's alley". Aerosmith lead guitarist <a href="/wiki/Joe_Perry_(musician)" title="Joe Perry (musician)">Joe Perry</a> was initially skeptical of the collaboration with Underwood, but after hearing it, said "Their voices matched up really well. It doesn't sound forced. It was just right."<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
