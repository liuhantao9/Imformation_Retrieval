	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Suriname Cricket Board</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"><span class="mw-redirectedfrom">&#160;&#160;(Redirected from <a href="/w/index.php?title=Surinaamse_Cricket_Bond&amp;redirect=no" class="mw-redirect" title="Surinaamse Cricket Bond">Surinaamse Cricket Bond</a>)</span></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Suriname Cricket Board</b> (<a href="/wiki/Dutch_language" title="Dutch language">Dutch</a>: <i>Surinaamse Cricket Bond</i>; <b>SCB</b>) is the official <a href="/wiki/Sport_governing_body" class="mw-redirect" title="Sport governing body">governing body</a> of the sport of <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricket</a> in <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>. Its headquarters are located in <a href="/wiki/Paramaribo" title="Paramaribo">Paramaribo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>. The SCB is Suriname's representative at the <a href="/wiki/International_Cricket_Council" title="International Cricket Council">International Cricket Council</a>, and has been an associate member of that body since 2002. It is included in the <a href="/wiki/ICC_Americas" title="ICC Americas">ICC Americas</a> development region.
</p>
