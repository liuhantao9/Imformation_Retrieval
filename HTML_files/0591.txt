<p><a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a> is located in the northern part of <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a> and is part of <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_South_America" title="Caribbean South America">Caribbean South America</a>, bordering the North <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a>, between <a href="/wiki/French_Guiana" title="French Guiana">French Guiana</a> and <a href="/wiki/Guyana" title="Guyana">Guyana</a>. It is mostly covered by tropical rainforest, containing a great diversity of flora and fauna that, for the most part, are increasingly threatened by new development. There is a relatively small population, most of which live along the coast.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate_and_terrain">Climate and terrain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Geography_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Climate and terrain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Terrain">Terrain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Geography_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Terrain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Most of the country is made up of rolling hills, but there is a narrow coastal plain that has swampy terrain.
</p><p><b>Elevation extremes</b>
</p><p><i>Lowest point:</i> Unnamed location in the coastal plain - 2 meters (6.6&#160;ft) below Sea Level.
<br /><i>Highest point:</i> <a href="/wiki/Juliana_Top" class="mw-redirect" title="Juliana Top">Juliana Top</a> - 1,230 meters (4,040&#160;ft)
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Natural_resources">Natural resources</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Geography_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Natural resources">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Timber, hydropower, fish, forests, hydroelectric potential, kaolin, shrimp, bauxite and gold. Small amounts of nickel, copper, platinum and iron ore. It also has sizeable oil.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Water">Water</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Geography_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Water">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The country has one large <a href="/wiki/Reservoir" title="Reservoir">reservoir</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Brokopondo_Reservoir" title="Brokopondo Reservoir">Brokopondo Reservoir</a>. Several rivers run through it, including the <a href="/wiki/Suriname_River" title="Suriname River">Suriname River</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nickerie_River" title="Nickerie River">Nickerie River</a> and <a href="/wiki/Maroni_River" class="mw-redirect" title="Maroni River">Maroni</a> or Marowijne River.
</p>

<p>Tropical Showers, no hurricanes.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (December 2017)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Environment">Environment</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Geography_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Environment">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Current_issues">Current issues</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Geography_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Current issues">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Deforestation is a real problem as timber is cut for export. There is also a lot of pollution of inland waterways by small-scale mining activities.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="International_agreements">International agreements</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Geography_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: International agreements">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Suriname has agreed to the following agreements:
<a href="/wiki/Biodiversity" title="Biodiversity">Biodiversity</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Nations_Framework_Convention_on_Climate_Change" title="United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change">Climate Change</a>, <a href="/wiki/Endangered_Species" class="mw-redirect" title="Endangered Species">Endangered Species</a>, <a href="/wiki/Law_of_the_Sea" class="mw-redirect" title="Law of the Sea">Law of the Sea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marine_Dumping" class="mw-redirect" title="Marine Dumping">Marine Dumping</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ozone_Layer_Protection" class="mw-redirect" title="Ozone Layer Protection">Ozone Layer Protection</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ship_Pollution" class="mw-redirect" title="Ship Pollution">Ship Pollution</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tropical_Timber_94" class="mw-redirect" title="Tropical Timber 94">Tropical Timber 94</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wetlands" class="mw-redirect" title="Wetlands">Wetlands</a>, <a href="/wiki/Whaling" title="Whaling">Whaling</a>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Extreme_points">Extreme points</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Geography_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Extreme points">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Northernmost point – <a href="/wiki/Oostelijke_Polders" title="Oostelijke Polders">Oostelijke Polders</a></li>
<li>Southernmost point – Border with <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> <a href="/wiki/Coeroeni" title="Coeroeni">Coeroeni</a></li>
<li>Westernmost point – Border with <a href="/wiki/Guyana" title="Guyana">Guyana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sipaliwini_District" title="Sipaliwini District">Sipaliwini District</a></li>
<li>Easternmost point – Border with <a href="/wiki/French_Guiana" title="French Guiana">French Guiana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sipaliwini_District" title="Sipaliwini District">Sipaliwini District</a></li>
<li>Highest point – <a href="/wiki/Julianatop" title="Julianatop">Julianatop</a>: 1,230 m</li>
<li>Lowest point – unnamed location on the coastal plain: -2 m</li></ul>
