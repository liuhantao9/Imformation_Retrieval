<p><i><b>Jericho</b></i> is a period drama mini-series created and written by <a href="/wiki/Stephen_Thompson_(writer)" title="Stephen Thompson (writer)">Steve Thompson</a> and directed by Paul Whittington.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The eight-part series premiered on <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_network)" title="ITV (TV network)">ITV</a> between 7 January and 25 February 2016.
</p><p>It is set in the fictional town of Jericho, a <a href="/wiki/Shanty_town" title="Shanty town">shanty town</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire_Dales" title="Yorkshire Dales">Yorkshire Dales</a> of England, which springs up around the construction of a railway <a href="/wiki/Viaduct" title="Viaduct">viaduct</a> in the 1870s. The series re-imagines the story of the building of the <a href="/wiki/Ribblehead_Viaduct" title="Ribblehead Viaduct">Ribblehead Viaduct</a>, which is renamed the Culverdale Viaduct in the show.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In April 2016 ITV confirmed that a second series of the show was not going to be commissioned.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The series is available via streaming video in the USA on <a href="/wiki/Acorn_TV" title="Acorn TV">Acorn TV</a>, beginning 11 July 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Premise">Premise</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jericho_(2016_TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Premise">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The series focuses upon the shanty town of Jericho, home to a community that will live, thrive and die in the shadow of the viaduct they have been brought together to build.
</p>

<tr class="vevent" style="text-align:center;background:#F2F2F2"><th scope="row" rowspan="1" id="ep7" style="text-align:center">7</th><td class="summary" style="text-align:left">"Episode 7"</td><td style="text-align:center">David Moore</td><td style="text-align:center">Caroline Henry</td><td style="text-align:center">18&#160;February&#160;2016<span style="display:none">&#160;(<span class="bday dtstart published updated">2016-02-18</span>)</span></td><td style="text-align:center">3.70</td></tr><tr class="expand-child"><td class="description" colspan="7" style="border-bottom:solid 3px #46352B">
Johnny is imprisoned by Coates who refuses to send for a magistrate as the navvies are goaded by one of their number to hang Johnny for the murder of Red Killeen. Coates arranges a hearing in the bar that he can manipulate to ensure Johnny's hanging despite knowing who the real killer is. Annie is cross examined and her daughter, Martha, denounces Johnny. Charles and Isabella head to the camp in time to hear the real killer confess.</td></tr>
<tr class="vevent" style="text-align:center;background:#F2F2F2"><th scope="row" rowspan="1" id="ep8" style="text-align:center">8</th><td class="summary" style="text-align:left">"Episode 8"</td><td style="text-align:center">David Moore</td><td style="text-align:center">Steve Thompson</td><td style="text-align:center">25&#160;February&#160;2016<span style="display:none">&#160;(<span class="bday dtstart published updated">2016-02-25</span>)</span></td><td style="text-align:center">3.71</td></tr><tr class="expand-child"><td class="description" colspan="7" style="border-bottom:solid 3px #46352B">
Johnny fires Coates. Intending to quarry their own stone a mine is constructed. During an inspection and subsequent cave-ins, Johnny, Charles, and Dagger are trapped. Coates has Davey Sharpe get gunpowder from the store using a key he had. The gunpowder effects a rescue but Charles is badly injured and Isabella needs Johnny's help to run the business. Annie is not happy but refuses Johnny's offer of marriage. Davey Sharpe asks Coates why he had a key to the gunpowder store. Later that evening as Davey walks to his wedding he is shot.</td></tr></tbody></table>
