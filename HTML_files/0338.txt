<p><b>Julianatop</b> is the highest mountain in <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a> at 1,280 metres (4,199&#160;ft). It is located in the <a href="/wiki/Sipaliwini_District" title="Sipaliwini District">Sipaliwini District</a>.
</p>
