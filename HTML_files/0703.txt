<p>The <b>President Pro Tempore of the Union of South American Nations</b> is the head position and representative of the <a href="/wiki/Union_of_South_American_Nations" title="Union of South American Nations">Union of South American Nations</a> (UNASUR). At international meetings, the declarations and concerted opinions of the supranational organism are presented by the President Pro Tempore. The office is exercised for a one-year period on a <a href="/wiki/Pro_tempore" title="Pro tempore">pro tempore</a> basis by one of the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_state" title="Head of state">heads of state</a> of each <a href="/wiki/Member_State_of_the_Union_of_South_American_Nations" class="mw-redirect" title="Member State of the Union of South American Nations">UNASUR Member State</a>, the succession following alphabetical order.<sup id="cite_ref-tratado_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tratado-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The most recent president was the <a href="/wiki/President_of_Bolivia" title="President of Bolivia">President of Bolivia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Evo_Morales" title="Evo Morales">Evo Morales</a>, who served, as acknowledged by unasursg.org, between 17 April 2018 and April 16, 2019.
</p><p>The attributions of the President Pro Tempore are:<sup id="cite_ref-tratado_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tratado-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>To organize, invite members, and preside over UNASUR meetings, present to the Council of Ministers of Foreign Affairs the annual plan of activities of UNASUR and the meetings agenda, in coordination with the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Secretaries_General_of_UNASUR" class="mw-redirect" title="List of Secretaries General of UNASUR">Secretary General</a>, represent UNASUR in international meetings, with the approval of the <a href="/wiki/Member_State_of_the_Union_of_South_American_Nations" class="mw-redirect" title="Member State of the Union of South American Nations">Member States</a> and sign declarations and agreements with third parties, after approval from the pertaining Unasur institutions.
</p><p>The presidency pro témpore directs the eight ministerial councils of the Unasur: South American Council of Health, South American Council of Social Development, South American Council of Infrastructure and Planning, South American Council of Education, Culture, Science, Technology and Innovation; South American Council of Fight against the South American Narcotráfico, Council of Defense South American, South American Council of Economy and Finances, and South American Council.
</p>
