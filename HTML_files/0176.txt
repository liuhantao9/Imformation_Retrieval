<p><b>Vice President of Suriname</b> (<a href="/wiki/Dutch_language" title="Dutch language">Dutch</a>: <i lang="nl">Vicepresident van de Republiek Suriname</i>) is the second-highest political position in <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>, after the <a href="/wiki/President_of_Suriname" title="President of Suriname">President</a>. The President and the Vice President are elected by the National Assembly for five-year terms.
</p><p>The position of Vice President was created in the Constitution of 1987, when the position of <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Suriname" class="mw-redirect" title="Prime Minister of Suriname">Prime Minister of Suriname</a> was abolished. The Vice President is charged with the day-to-day management of the Council of Ministers<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and is responsible to the President.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Ashwin_Adhin" title="Ashwin Adhin">Ashwin Adhin</a> has been the incumbent Vice President of Suriname since August 12, 2015.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Powers_and_duties">Powers and duties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vice_President_of_Suriname&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Powers and duties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The powers of the President are exercised by the Vice-President:
</p>
<ol><li>In case the President is declared unfit to exercise his powers;</li>
<li>In case the President has laid down the exercise of his powers temporarily;</li>
<li>As long as there is no President or if he is absent;</li>
<li>If, in the case described in article 140, prosecution against the President has been initiated.</li></ol>
