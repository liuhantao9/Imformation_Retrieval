<p><b>White Surinamese</b> or <b>European Surinamese</b> are <a href="/wiki/Demographics_of_Suriname" title="Demographics of Suriname">Surinamese</a> people whose ancestry lies within the continent of <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>.
</p><p>As of 2013, people of solely European descent are a small minority in <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>, accounting for only 1% of the country's population. The largest European ethnic groups in Suriname are the Dutch and the Portuguese.<sup id="cite_ref-cia_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cia-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
