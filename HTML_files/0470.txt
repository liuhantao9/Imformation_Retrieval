<p>The <b>5th <a href="/wiki/IAAF_World_Championships_in_Athletics" class="mw-redirect" title="IAAF World Championships in Athletics">World Championships in Athletics</a></b>, under the auspices of the <a href="/wiki/International_Association_of_Athletics_Federations" title="International Association of Athletics Federations">International Association of Athletics Federations</a>, were held at the <a href="/wiki/Ullevi" title="Ullevi">Ullevi Stadium</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gothenburg" title="Gothenburg">Gothenburg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a> on 5–13 August 1995.
</p><p>This edition featured 1804 athletes from 191 nations.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This competition saw the women run the 5000 m event at the World Championships for the first time. The race replaced the 3000 m event which had been run at all previous World Championships.
</p>
