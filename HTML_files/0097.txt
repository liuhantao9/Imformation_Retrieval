<p>"<b>The Fighter</b>" is a song co-written and recorded by New Zealand-born Australian <a href="/wiki/Country_music" title="Country music">country</a> singer <a href="/wiki/Keith_Urban" title="Keith Urban">Keith Urban</a> featuring <a href="/wiki/Carrie_Underwood" title="Carrie Underwood">Carrie Underwood</a> as a duet. It was released on 6 February 2017 as the fifth and final single from Urban's 2016 album, <i><a href="/wiki/Ripcord_(album)" title="Ripcord (album)">Ripcord</a></i>. Urban co-wrote and co-produced this song with <a href="/wiki/Busbee" title="Busbee">busbee</a>.
</p><p>The song has entered the record charts of Australia, Canada, the United States, Belgium, and Scotland, where it became Urban's first song to enter the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Singles_Chart" class="mw-redirect" title="Scottish Singles Chart">Scottish</a> and Belgian charts.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Content">Content</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Fighter_(Keith_Urban_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Content">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The song is set in the key of <a href="/wiki/B_minor" title="B minor">B minor</a> with a main chord pattern of G<sub>maj</sub>7–A<sub>sus</sub>4–F<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode;"><span class="music-sharp">&#x266f;</span></span><sub>m</sub>7–Bm and an approximate tempo of 132 <a href="/wiki/Beats_per_minute" class="mw-redirect" title="Beats per minute">beats per minute</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is about a man promising that he will defend and support a woman.
</p><p>Urban said of the song's evolution as "I was working with <a href="/wiki/Busbee" title="Busbee">busbee</a> that day; we were in London. I was actually driving to the studio to work on this other song we started, and I had most of it the chorus in my head, and it felt like I had most of the song in my head — the verses just had to flesh out. I walked into the studio and played the chord progression, and he built this track really quickly, and then I could sing over the top of it". He continued and said "it was just a very quick, quick song to write, because I literally thought about <a href="/wiki/Nicole_Kidman" title="Nicole Kidman">Nic</a> and I and our relationship in the beginning, and some of the things we had said all went into that song."<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commercial_performance">Commercial performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Fighter_(Keith_Urban_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Commercial performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Before_single_release">Before single release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Fighter_(Keith_Urban_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Before single release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Following the release of the album, "The Fighter" sold 25,000 copies in the first week and debuted at number 25 on the <a href="/wiki/Hot_Country_Songs" title="Hot Country Songs">Hot Country Songs</a> chart and also at number 18 on the <a href="/wiki/Bubbling_Under_Hot_100_Singles" class="mw-redirect" title="Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles">Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles</a> chart. In the next week, the song jumped to number 11 on the Hot Country Songs chart and also debuted at number 69 on the US <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a>, selling 50,000 copies.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It debuted on the Canadian Hot 100 at number 75.
</p><p>In Australia, the song debuted at number 30 on the <a href="/wiki/ARIA_Charts" title="ARIA Charts">ARIA Charts</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> becoming Urban's seventh top 50 entry and also his third highest-charting song on the chart. For the week of 10 July 2016, the song climbed from number 27 to number 19, becoming its new peak. The song has been certified Platinum, denoting sales of over 70,000 copies in Australia.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> It is Urban's third top twenty hit and Underwood's second entry on the ARIA Charts and also became her highest-charting song on that chart.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="After_single_release">After single release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Fighter_(Keith_Urban_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: After single release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The song was released as the fifth official single from <i>Ripcord</i> on 6 February 2017. It debuted at number 44 on the US <a href="/wiki/Country_Airplay" title="Country Airplay">Country Airplay</a> chart and reached number 2, becoming Urban's 38th consecutive top ten hit which is the longest running streak in that chart's history. It became Underwood's 26th consecutive top ten hit, which is the second longest active streak of country airplay top tens.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> It re-entered the <a href="/wiki/Hot_Country_Songs" title="Hot Country Songs">Hot Country Songs</a> chart at number five, selling 45,000 copies in its week of release and peaked at number two. It also re-entered the US <i>Billboard</i> Hot 100 at number 53 and has reached number 38, becoming Urban's twentieth top 40 hit and Underwood's twenty first, and her first since "<a href="/wiki/Something_in_the_Water_(Carrie_Underwood_song)" title="Something in the Water (Carrie Underwood song)">Something in the Water</a>" peaked at number 24 in October 2014.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> On the Canada Country chart, the song has reached number 2 and peaked at number 65 on the Canadian Hot 100. The song has been certified Platinum in Canada for shipments of 80,000 copies.<sup id="cite_ref-MusicCanada_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MusicCanada-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> It entered the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Singles_Chart" class="mw-redirect" title="Scottish Singles Chart">Scottish Singles Chart</a> at number 52, becoming Urban's first and Underwood's fourth entry on that chart following "<a href="/wiki/Blown_Away_(Carrie_Underwood_song)" title="Blown Away (Carrie Underwood song)">Blown Away</a>", "<a href="/wiki/Something_in_the_Water_(Carrie_Underwood_song)" title="Something in the Water (Carrie Underwood song)">Something in the Water</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Heartbeat_(Carrie_Underwood_song)" title="Heartbeat (Carrie Underwood song)">Heartbeat</a>".
</p><p>As of April 2018, it has sold a total of 888,000 copies in the US.<sup id="cite_ref-us_sales_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-us_sales-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The song was certified Double Platinum by the <a href="/wiki/RIAA" class="mw-redirect" title="RIAA">RIAA</a> on August 23, 2019, denoting sales of over 2,000,000 units in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Fighter_(Keith_Urban_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Urban shared an exclusive 24-hour first look video for the song through his Vevo account. Directed by John Urbano, the video features the two singing the song in a boxing ring, intercut with various people dancing to the song in various outdoor locations at night.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The video was later taken down from YouTube after the initial 24 hours.
</p><p>The video was again uploaded to YouTube on 16 February 2017.
</p><p>On 2 April 2017, another version of the video (featuring only the dancing sequences) was uploaded to Urban's YouTube account.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Live_performances">Live performances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Fighter_(Keith_Urban_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Live performances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Urban and Underwood performed the song live at the <a href="/wiki/59th_Annual_Grammy_Awards" title="59th Annual Grammy Awards">59th Grammy Awards</a> on February 12, 2017. The duo later performed the song at the 52nd <a href="/wiki/52nd_Academy_of_Country_Music_Awards" title="52nd Academy of Country Music Awards">Academy of Country Music Awards</a> on April 2, 2017. On June 7, 2017, the singers once more paired up and performed the song at the 2017 <a href="/wiki/CMT_Music_Awards" title="CMT Music Awards">CMT Music Awards</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
