	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Système universitaire de documentation</h1>

<p>The <b>système universitaire de documentation</b> or <b>SUDOC</b> is a system used by the libraries of <a href="/wiki/Higher_education_in_France" class="mw-redirect" title="Higher education in France">French universities and higher education establishments</a> to identify, track and manage the <a href="/wiki/Records_management" title="Records management">documents</a> in their possession. The catalog, which contains more than 10 million references, allows students and researchers to search for bibliographical and location information in over 3,400 documentation centers. It is maintained by the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Bibliographic_Agency_for_Higher_Education&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Bibliographic Agency for Higher Education (page does not exist)">Bibliographic Agency for Higher Education</a><span class="noprint" style="font-size:85%; font-style: normal;">&#160;&#91;<a href="https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agence_bibliographique_de_l%27enseignement_sup%C3%A9rieur" class="extiw" title="fr:Agence bibliographique de l&#39;enseignement supérieur">fr</a>&#93;</span> (ABES).
</p>
