<p><b>Tafelberg</b> (literally "<a href="/wiki/Table_Mountain" title="Table Mountain">Table Mountain</a>") is one of the highest mountains in <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a> at 1,026 metres (3,366&#160;ft). It is a <a href="/wiki/Tepui" title="Tepui">tepui</a> and is part of the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Tafelberg_Nature_Reserve&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Tafelberg Nature Reserve (page does not exist)">Tafelberg Nature Reserve</a>. The mountain is in the <a href="/wiki/Sipaliwini_District" title="Sipaliwini District">Sipaliwini District</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Tafelberg_Airstrip" title="Tafelberg Airstrip">Tafelberg Airstrip</a> is nearby.
</p>
