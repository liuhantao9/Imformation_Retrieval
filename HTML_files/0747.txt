<p>The <b>Secretariat for Political Affairs of the Organization of American States</b> is a part of the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Organization_of_American_States_General_Secretariat&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Organization of American States General Secretariat (page does not exist)">General Secretariat</a>. The Secretariat for Political Affairs has a mandate to strengthen the political processes of the Organization of American States member states and to increase the legitimacy of those political institutions.<sup id="cite_ref-OASSPA1_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OASSPA1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Structure">Structure</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Organization_of_American_States_Secretariat_for_Political_Affairs&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Structure">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Secretariat for Political Affairs consists of the Executive Office of the Secretary for Political Affairs, an administrative support section, and three departments.<sup id="cite_ref-OASSPA1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OASSPA1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Department_of_Electoral_Cooperation_and_Observation">Department of Electoral Cooperation and Observation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Organization_of_American_States_Secretariat_for_Political_Affairs&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Department of Electoral Cooperation and Observation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.oas.org/en/spa/deco/default.asp">Department of Electoral Cooperation and Observation (DECO)</a> provides technical assistance for electoral activities for member states. The Department of Electoral Cooperation also maintains an Electoral Observation Missions Sections to provide the Organization of American States with <a href="/wiki/Election_monitoring" title="Election monitoring">election monitoring</a> capabilities.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Department_of_Sustainable_Democracy_and_Special_Missions">Department of Sustainable Democracy and Special Missions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Organization_of_American_States_Secretariat_for_Political_Affairs&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Department of Sustainable Democracy and Special Missions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.oas.org/en/spa/dsdsm/">Department of Sustainable Democracy and Special Missions (DSDSM)</a> provides support to the General Secretariat in handling political and institutional conflicts in the region.
</p><p>The Department carries out efforts in three interrelated areas to prevent, manage, and resolve conflicts. It implements a methodology for political analysis; provides advice and technical support to missions established by the Permanent Council or the OAS Secretary General; and finally, coordinates activities geared toward the peaceful settlement of territorial disputes and the promotion of a culture of peace.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Department_for_Effective_Public_Management">Department for Effective Public Management</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Organization_of_American_States_Secretariat_for_Political_Affairs&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Department for Effective Public Management">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.oas.org/en/spa/depm/default.asp">Department for Effective Public Management (DEPM)</a> provides support to OAS Member States in matters of public management through strengthening democratic institutions. This includes technical assistance projects in organizational design and results based management; <a href="/wiki/Information_and_communications_technology" title="Information and communications technology">information and communication technology (ICT)</a> application to public sector operation; training programs and mechanisms for dialogue and experiences exchange that promote horizontal cooperation.
</p>
