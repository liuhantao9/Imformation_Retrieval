<p>The <a href="/wiki/9th_European_Parliament" class="mw-redirect" title="9th European Parliament">9th European Parliament</a> was <a href="/wiki/2019_European_Parliament_election" title="2019 European Parliament election">elected</a> across the European Union in the late days of May 2019 for the 2019–2024 session. In the United Kingdom the election took place on 23 May. It is not known for how long British MEPs will sit because of the uncertainties over Brexit.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
