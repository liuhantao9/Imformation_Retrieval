<p>The <b>Charter of the Organization of the American States</b> (otherwise known the <b>Charter of the OAS</b>) is a Pan-American treaty that sets out the creation of the <a href="/wiki/Organization_of_American_States" title="Organization of American States">Organization of American States</a>. It was signed at the Ninth <a href="/wiki/International_Conference_of_American_States" class="mw-redirect" title="International Conference of American States">International Conference of American States</a> of 30 April 1948, held in <a href="/wiki/Bogot%C3%A1" title="Bogotá">Bogotá</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a>. The treaty came into effect on 13 December 1951.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Amendments">Amendments</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Charter_of_the_Organization_of_American_States&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Amendments">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It has been amended by the:
</p>
<ul><li>Protocol of Buenos Aires (27 February 1967)</li>
<li>Protocol of Cartagena (5 December 1985)</li>
<li>Protocol of Washington (14 December 1992)</li>
<li>Protocol of Managua (10 June 1992)</li></ul>
