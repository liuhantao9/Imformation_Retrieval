<p><b>Stotts Coaches</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> is a bus and coach company based in <a href="/wiki/Milnsbridge" title="Milnsbridge">Milnsbridge</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stotts_Coaches&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:KX07HDN_E_Stott_%5E_Sons,Milnsbridge._Volvo_B12M_Plaxton_Panther._12_March_2011_-_Flickr_-_sludgegulper.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/KX07HDN_E_Stott_%5E_Sons%2CMilnsbridge._Volvo_B12M_Plaxton_Panther._12_March_2011_-_Flickr_-_sludgegulper.jpg/220px-KX07HDN_E_Stott_%5E_Sons%2CMilnsbridge._Volvo_B12M_Plaxton_Panther._12_March_2011_-_Flickr_-_sludgegulper.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/KX07HDN_E_Stott_%5E_Sons%2CMilnsbridge._Volvo_B12M_Plaxton_Panther._12_March_2011_-_Flickr_-_sludgegulper.jpg/330px-KX07HDN_E_Stott_%5E_Sons%2CMilnsbridge._Volvo_B12M_Plaxton_Panther._12_March_2011_-_Flickr_-_sludgegulper.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/KX07HDN_E_Stott_%5E_Sons%2CMilnsbridge._Volvo_B12M_Plaxton_Panther._12_March_2011_-_Flickr_-_sludgegulper.jpg/440px-KX07HDN_E_Stott_%5E_Sons%2CMilnsbridge._Volvo_B12M_Plaxton_Panther._12_March_2011_-_Flickr_-_sludgegulper.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2133" data-file-height="1600" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:KX07HDN_E_Stott_%5E_Sons,Milnsbridge._Volvo_B12M_Plaxton_Panther._12_March_2011_-_Flickr_-_sludgegulper.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Plaxton_Panther" title="Plaxton Panther">Plaxton Panther</a> bodied <a href="/wiki/Volvo_B12M" title="Volvo B12M">Volvo B12M</a> in March 2011</div></div></div>
<p>Stotts Coaches has been providing services in <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield" title="Huddersfield">Huddersfield</a> and the wider <a href="/wiki/Kirklees" title="Kirklees">Kirklees</a> area for over 50 years.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The company provides coach services for schools in Kirklees along with day trips and private coach hire. The company also runs coach journeys for the town's <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Town_F.C" class="mw-redirect" title="Huddersfield Town F.C">football</a> and <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Giants" title="Huddersfield Giants">rugby league</a> teams.
</p><p>Stotts Coaches provide local bus services in the Huddersfield area serving Huddersfield town centre, <a href="/wiki/Brighouse" title="Brighouse">Brighouse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dalton,_West_Yorkshire" class="mw-redirect" title="Dalton, West Yorkshire">Dalton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Holmfirth" title="Holmfirth">Holmfirth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Meltham" title="Meltham">Meltham</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slaithwaite" title="Slaithwaite">Slaithwaite</a>. These services, along with the school bus service, are run on behalf of the <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire_Passenger_Transport_Executive" class="mw-redirect" title="West Yorkshire Passenger Transport Executive">West Yorkshire Passenger Transport Executive</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Services">Services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stotts_Coaches&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Services">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="National_Express">National Express</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stotts_Coaches&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: National Express">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Stotts Coaches are a <a href="/wiki/National_Express_Coaches" title="National Express Coaches">National Express</a> contractor and operate services on routes:<sup id="cite_ref-NXHandbook_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NXHandbook-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li>310 <a href="/wiki/Bradford" title="Bradford">Bradford</a> - <a href="/wiki/Leicester" title="Leicester">Leicester</a></li>
<li>335 <a href="/wiki/Halifax,_West_Yorkshire" title="Halifax, West Yorkshire">Halifax</a> - <a href="/wiki/Birmingham" title="Birmingham">Birmingham</a></li>
<li>351 <a href="/wiki/Sheffield" title="Sheffield">Sheffield</a> - <a href="/wiki/Blackpool" title="Blackpool">Blackpool</a></li>
<li>542 Blackpool - <a href="/wiki/Glasgow" title="Glasgow">Glasgow</a></li>
<li>561 Bradford - <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a></li>
<li>660 Bradford - <a href="/wiki/Ingoldmells" title="Ingoldmells">Ingoldmells</a></li></ul>
