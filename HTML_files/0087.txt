<p>"<b>Dirty Laundry</b>" is a song recorded by American singer and songwriter <a href="/wiki/Carrie_Underwood" title="Carrie Underwood">Carrie Underwood</a> from her fifth studio album, <i><a href="/wiki/Storyteller_(Carrie_Underwood_album)" title="Storyteller (Carrie Underwood album)">Storyteller</a></i> (2015). The song was written by <a href="/wiki/Zach_Crowell" title="Zach Crowell">Zach Crowell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ashley_Gorley" title="Ashley Gorley">Ashley Gorley</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hillary_Lindsey" title="Hillary Lindsey">Hillary Lindsey</a>, with production from Jay Joyce, and was released  as the fourth single from the album in the United States and Canada.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The single was shipped to country radio on August 22 and made its official impact on September 5.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Composition_and_lyrics">Composition and lyrics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dirty_Laundry_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Composition and lyrics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Dirty Laundry" is a song about <a href="/wiki/Infidelity" title="Infidelity">infidelity</a>. About the song, co-writer Ashley Gorley said, "Zach's tracks have a theme of being a little wild and dark and crazy...He was playing these tracks we went through, and all I remember is stumbling into the <a href="/wiki/Ajax_(cleanser)" class="mw-redirect" title="Ajax (cleanser)">Ajax</a> line, and that sounding awesome."<sup id="cite_ref-auto_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-auto-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Co-writer Hillary Lindsey said of the song, "I was so sick … They made me sing it, and I was mortified. We turned it in, and I remember Chris Oglesby [BMG vice president, creative] and Daniel Lee [BMG senior creative director] saying it was one of their favorite songs I had turned in in a while, and how great it sounded, and I was so sick."<sup id="cite_ref-auto_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-auto-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>"It's a take on a cheater in a familiar way, with the lipstick on the collar and the red wine, and there's all these tell-tale signs that he was with another woman, and it shows on his dirty laundry," Underwood said of the song. "But it's also like she airs it out for the whole world to see."<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Producer and co-writer Zach Crowell said of the experience, "There was no talk of murder in this song. I wrote "<a href="/wiki/Church_Bells_(song)" title="Church Bells (song)">Church Bells</a>" with Hillary. I was lucky enough to write that song as well. That was the first song I’ve ever killed someone in the song. It was cool to do it, [because] we knew we were kind of taking a shot at a Carrie song, and we knew, 'Oh, she would like that, if we killed this guy.' She's not afraid to murder people in songs, but we did not kill him in "Dirty Laundry." We just announced it to the whole town."<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Critical_reception">Critical reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dirty_Laundry_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Critical reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Dirty Laundry" was met with positive reviews from critics. Sarah Rodman of <a href="/wiki/The_Boston_Globe" title="The Boston Globe">The Boston Globe</a> magazine reviewed the song favorably, calling it "one of the best six tracks on the album", and noted it as the album highlight.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Idolator_(website)" title="Idolator (website)">Idolator</a> praised the song stating it as "a remarkable one-two punch that blends clever lyrics with the muscular snap of arena country-rock".<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><i>Taste of Country</i> reviewed the song, saying, "Musically this song relies on the stormy production she leaned into heavily on her <i>Blown Away</i> album. There’s a colorful thread that’s tied everything Underwood has done over the last five years together and she hasn’t been afraid to lean into it again and again."<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <i>AXS</i> gave a positive review of the song, writing, "By far, "Dirty Laundry" is one of the singer's boldest and most musically adventurous singles of her career. There is an intensity and fury as inescapable as "<a href="/wiki/Blown_Away_(Carrie_Underwood_song)" title="Blown Away (Carrie Underwood song)">Blown Away</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Two_Black_Cadillacs" title="Two Black Cadillacs">Two Black Cadillacs</a>," but the story is fresh and windswept."<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commercial_performance">Commercial performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dirty_Laundry_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Commercial performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Following the release of the album,  "Dirty Laundry" debuted at number 47 on the US Hot Country Songs chart for the week ending November 14, 2015,<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> selling 3,000 copies.
</p><p>After being released as the album's fourth single, the song debuted at number 51 on the <a href="/wiki/Country_Airplay" title="Country Airplay">Country Airplay</a> chart<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> and reached number two. It re-entered the <a href="/wiki/Hot_Country_Songs" title="Hot Country Songs">Hot Country Songs</a> chart at number 35 and reached a peak of number three. "Dirty Laundry" marks Underwood's twenty-fifth top ten single on the country charts.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>On the chart dated November 5, 2016, it entered the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a> at number 73 and peaked at number 48.
</p><p>In Canada, the song debuted at number 50 on the Canada Country chart and has reached number one on the chart dated December 31, 2016. It also debuted at number 99 on the Canadian Hot 100 chart and reached number 71.
</p><p>As of March 2017, it has sold 271,000 copies in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dirty_Laundry_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The music video was directed by <a href="/wiki/Shane_Drake" title="Shane Drake">Shane Drake</a> and premiered in October 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Live_performances">Live performances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dirty_Laundry_(Carrie_Underwood_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Live performances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Underwood first performed the song live at the release party for the <i>Storyteller</i> album, at the iHeartRadio Theater in New York City.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> She has since performed the song live numerous times for the <a href="/wiki/Storyteller_Tour:_Stories_in_the_Round" title="Storyteller Tour: Stories in the Round">Storyteller Tour</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Underwood performed the song live on <i><a href="/wiki/The_Ellen_DeGeneres_Show" title="The Ellen DeGeneres Show">The Ellen DeGeneres Show</a></i> on September 16.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Her next televised performance came at the 50th annual <a href="/wiki/Country_Music_Association" title="Country Music Association">CMA</a> Awards, where she performed "Dirty Laundry" with an all-female band that included <a href="/wiki/Orianthi" title="Orianthi">Orianthi</a>. <sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
