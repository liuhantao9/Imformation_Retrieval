<p><b>Marowijne</b> is a district of <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>, located on the north-east coast. Marowijne's <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital city</a> is <a href="/wiki/Albina,_Suriname" title="Albina, Suriname">Albina</a>, with other towns including <a href="/wiki/Moengo" title="Moengo">Moengo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wanhatti" title="Wanhatti">Wanhatti</a>. The district borders the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a> to the north, <a href="/wiki/French_Guiana" title="French Guiana">French Guiana</a> to the east, the Surinamese district of <a href="/wiki/Sipaliwini" title="Sipaliwini">Sipaliwini</a> to the south, and the Surinamese districts of <a href="/wiki/Commewijne_District" title="Commewijne District">Commewijne</a> and <a href="/wiki/Para_District" title="Para District">Para</a> to the west.
</p><p>Marowijne's <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> is 20,250 and the district has an <a href="/wiki/Area" title="Area">area</a> of 4,627&#160;km².
</p><p>During the early 20th century, <a href="/wiki/Bauxite" title="Bauxite">bauxite</a> was discovered in Marowijne district, leading to a boom in <a href="/wiki/Mining" title="Mining">mining</a> that significantly enriched the entire country. The district also has a large <a href="/wiki/Tourism" title="Tourism">tourism</a> industry and is the location of oceanfront resorts.
</p><p>Marowijne is also home to many <a href="/wiki/Maroon_(people)" title="Maroon (people)">Maroon</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Palenque_(village)" class="mw-redirect" title="Palenque (village)">palenques</a>.</i> The Maroons are descendants of <a href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">slaves</a> who escaped from their <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Dutch</a> masters centuries ago, particularly slaves who escaped from Suriname's huge <a href="/wiki/Plantation" title="Plantation">plantations</a> during the 18th century. They established autonomy for their interior settlements by a 1785 peace treaty with the Netherlands, which was unable to conquer them.
</p>
