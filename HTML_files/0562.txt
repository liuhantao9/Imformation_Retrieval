<p>The <b><a class="mw-selflink selflink">Ahmadiyya Anjuman Isha’at Islam</a> Mosque  Keizerstraat</b> (<span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Mosque_Keizerstraat&amp;params=5_49_43_N_55_9_36_W_type:landmark"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">5°49′43″N</span> <span class="longitude">55°9′36″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">&#xfeff; / &#xfeff;</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">5.82861°N 55.16000°W</span><span style="display:none">&#xfeff; / <span class="geo">5.82861; -55.16000</span></span></span></a></span>) is the headquarters of the <a href="/wiki/Lahore_Ahmadiyya_Movement_for_the_Propagation_of_Islam" title="Lahore Ahmadiyya Movement for the Propagation of Islam">Lahore Ahmadiyya Movement for the Propagation of Islam</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paramaribo" title="Paramaribo">Paramaribo</a> in <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>, “Surinaamse Islamitische Vereniging” (SIV)
</p><p>The mosque is located in the <a href="/wiki/Keizerstraat" title="Keizerstraat">Keizerstraat</a>, adjacent to the <a href="/wiki/Neveh_Shalom_Synagogue" title="Neveh Shalom Synagogue">Neveh Shalom Synagogue</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mosque_Keizerstraat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Muslim community of Paramaribo was established in 1929. Its first mosque, a wooden rectangular building with minarets, was completed in 1932. In 1979 boxing legend  <a href="/wiki/Muhammad_Ali" title="Muhammad Ali">Muhammad Ali</a> visited the mosque.
</p><p>The current mosque was completed in 1984.
</p>
