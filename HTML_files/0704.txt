<p>The <b>Secretary General of the Union of South American Nations</b> is the legal representative of the Secretariat of the <a href="/wiki/Union_of_South_American_Nations" title="Union of South American Nations">Union of South American Nations</a> (USAN).
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secretary_General_of_the_Union_of_South_American_Nations&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The position was established by the <a href="/wiki/UNASUR_Constitutive_Treaty" title="UNASUR Constitutive Treaty">UNASUR Constitutive Treaty</a> and the first Secretary General was designated on 4 May 2010.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Function">Function</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secretary_General_of_the_Union_of_South_American_Nations&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Function">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Secretary General of UNASUR is proposed by the Foreign Ministers council, and designated by the Heads of State and Government council<sup id="cite_ref-TCU_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TCU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> for a two-year term, renewable once.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> A Secretary General cannot be of the same nationality in succession.<sup id="cite_ref-TCU_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TCU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Secretary General exercises the legal representation of the UNASUR Secretariat. The officials selection for the latter requires an equitative representation between <a href="/wiki/Member_State_of_the_Union_of_South_American_Nations" class="mw-redirect" title="Member State of the Union of South American Nations">UNASUR Member States</a>. It also requires an equitative representation, as much as possible, along <a href="/wiki/Gender" title="Gender">gender</a>, language and ethnic criteria.<sup id="cite_ref-TCU_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TCU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
The tasks of the Secretary General are:<sup id="cite_ref-TCU_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TCU-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li>Helping the Heads of State council, the Foreign Ministers council, the Delegates council, and the <a href="/wiki/President_Pro_Tempore_of_UNASUR" class="mw-redirect" title="President Pro Tempore of UNASUR">Pro Tempore President</a>, in the accomplishment of their tasks.</li>
<li>Proposing initiatives and monitoring the adequate functioning of the UNASUR organizations.</li>
<li>Preparing and presenting the Annual Report, and respective reports, to UNASUR organizations meetings.</li>
<li>Serving as the legal depositary of UNASUR Agreements, and allowing its publication.</li>
<li>Preparing the annual budget for the Delegates Council consideration, and adopting measures for good management and administration, as needed.</li>
<li>Projecting reglamentations for the Secretariat, and submitting them to the pertaining bodies for consideration and approval.</li>
<li>Coordinating the labor bestowed on it by UNASUR organizations along with other Latin American and the Caribbean integration and cooperation entities.</li>
<li>Executing, according to reglamentations, every necessary legal action for the good management and administration of the Secretariat.</li></ul>
