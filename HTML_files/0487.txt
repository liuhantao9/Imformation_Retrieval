<p>The <b>Suriname national cricket team</b> is the team that represents the <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Suriname" class="mw-redirect" title="Republic of Suriname">Republic of Suriname</a> in <a href="/wiki/International_cricket" title="International cricket">international cricket</a>. The team is organised by the <a href="/wiki/Surinaamse_Cricket_Bond" class="mw-redirect" title="Surinaamse Cricket Bond">Surinaamse Cricket Bond</a>, which has been an <a href="/wiki/ICC_members" class="mw-redirect" title="ICC members">associate member</a> of the <a href="/wiki/International_Cricket_Council" title="International Cricket Council">International Cricket Council</a> (ICC) since 2011 (and previous an affiliate member from 2002).<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Suriname made its international debut at the 2004 Americas Affiliates Championship in Panama, where it placed last. At the <a href="/wiki/2006_ICC_Americas_Championship" title="2006 ICC Americas Championship">2006 ICC Americas Championship</a>, the team won the division three event, and was promoted to division two for the <a href="/wiki/2008_ICC_Americas_Championship" title="2008 ICC Americas Championship">2008 edition</a>. Suriname also won that event, and were promoted to the <a href="/wiki/2008_ICC_Americas_Championship_Division_One" title="2008 ICC Americas Championship Division One">2008 Division One</a> tournament in the United States, but were immediately relegated after going winless. Since then, Suriname has generally alternated between the first and second divisions of <a href="/wiki/ICC_Americas" title="ICC Americas">ICC Americas</a> events, most recently placing fourth (out of four teams) at the <a href="/wiki/2015_ICC_Americas_Twenty20_Division_One" title="2015 ICC Americas Twenty20 Division One">2015 Americas Twenty20 Championship</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the <a href="/wiki/World_Cricket_League" title="World Cricket League">World Cricket League</a> (WCL), the team placed last in the <a href="/wiki/2009_ICC_World_Cricket_League_Division_Seven" title="2009 ICC World Cricket League Division Seven">2009 Division Seven</a> tournament and fifth (out of eight teams) at the <a href="/wiki/2010_ICC_World_Cricket_League_Division_Eight" title="2010 ICC World Cricket League Division Eight">2010 Division Eight</a> tournament, relegating it to the regional qualifiers. Suriname re-entered the WCL system at the <a href="/wiki/2015_ICC_World_Cricket_League_Division_Six" title="2015 ICC World Cricket League Division Six">2015 Division Six</a> event in England, and subsequently qualified for <a href="/wiki/2016_ICC_World_Cricket_League_Division_Five" title="2016 ICC World Cricket League Division Five">2016 Division Five</a>. However, they later withdrew from the Division Five tournament due to an ICC investigation about the eligibility of some of their players.<sup id="cite_ref-Suriname_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Suriname-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-BBCSuriname_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSuriname-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In April 2018, the ICC decided to grant full <a href="/wiki/Twenty20_International" title="Twenty20 International">Twenty20 International</a> (T20I) status to all its members. Therefore, all <a href="/wiki/Twenty20" title="Twenty20">Twenty20</a> matches played between Suriname and other <a href="/wiki/List_of_International_Cricket_Council_members" title="List of International Cricket Council members">ICC members</a> after 1 January 2019 will be a full T20I.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 3rd May 2019, <a href="/wiki/International_Cricket_Council" title="International Cricket Council">International Cricket Council</a> release global rankings for <a href="/wiki/Twenty20_International" title="Twenty20 International">Twenty20 International</a> in which Suriname were placed on <b>35th rank</b>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Suriname_national_cricket_team&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<table class="box-Expand_section plainlinks metadata ambox mbox-small-left ambox-content" role="presentation"><tbody><tr><td class="mbox-image"><a href="/wiki/File:Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg" class="image"><img alt="[icon]" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/20px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png" decoding="async" width="20" height="14" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/30px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/40px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="44" data-file-height="31" /></a></td><td class="mbox-text"><div class="mbox-text-span">This section <b>needs expansion</b>. <small>You can help by <a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Suriname_national_cricket_team&amp;action=edit&amp;section=">adding to it</a>.</small>  <small class="date-container"><i>(<span class="date">August 2016</span>)</i></small></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
<p>Suriname became an affiliate member of the <a href="/wiki/International_Cricket_Council" title="International Cricket Council">International Cricket Council</a> in 2002. Their international debut came in 2004 at the Americas Affiliates Championship, where they came last in the five team tournament. A big improvement came two years later in the new Division Three of the <a href="/wiki/ICC_Americas_Championship" title="ICC Americas Championship">ICC Americas Championship</a> when they won a four team tournament also involving <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_cricket_team" class="mw-redirect" title="Brazilian cricket team">Brazil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chilean_cricket_team" class="mw-redirect" title="Chilean cricket team">Chile</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Turks_and_Caicos_Islands_cricket_team" class="mw-redirect" title="Turks and Caicos Islands cricket team">Turks and Caicos Islands</a>. This qualified them for the Division Two tournament played in <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a> late in 2006, when they came fourth out of five teams, which means that they will keep their Division Two place in 2008.
</p><p>Suriname placed fifth at the <a href="/wiki/2009_ICC_World_Cricket_League_Division_Seven" title="2009 ICC World Cricket League Division Seven">2009 Global Division Seven</a> tournament and was relegated to <a href="/wiki/2010_ICC_World_Cricket_League_Division_Eight" title="2010 ICC World Cricket League Division Eight">Division Eight</a>, where it placed fifth to again be relegated.
</p>
