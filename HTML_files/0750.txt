<p><b>Topman</b> is a British <a href="/wiki/Multinational_corporation" title="Multinational corporation">multinational</a> men's fashion retailer. Along with its women's clothing counterpart <a href="/wiki/Topshop" title="Topshop">Topshop</a>, Topman is a subsidiary of the <a href="/wiki/Arcadia_Group" title="Arcadia Group">Arcadia Group</a>, which also owns <a href="/wiki/Burton_(clothes_retailer)" class="mw-redirect" title="Burton (clothes retailer)">Burton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Miss_Selfridge" title="Miss Selfridge">Miss Selfridge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wallis_(retailer)" title="Wallis (retailer)">Wallis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Evans_(clothing)" class="mw-redirect" title="Evans (clothing)">Evans</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dorothy_Perkins" title="Dorothy Perkins">Dorothy Perkins</a>. Topman has a chain of <a href="/wiki/High-street" class="mw-redirect" title="High-street">high-street</a> men's clothing stores located throughout the United Kingdom and has a presence on just almost every UK High Street, and in Ireland main street outlets in six cities and towns. Topman stores almost always share their premises with Topshop. Topman was created in the UK towards the end of the 1970s to provide a new approach to menswear retailing. Since its creation, Topman has also built an international presence, as it continues to expand throughout the US, Europe, and Asia. The chain occupies two entire floors at the ‘world’s largest fashion shop’<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>, in <a href="/wiki/Oxford_Circus" class="mw-redirect" title="Oxford Circus">Oxford Circus</a>, London.
</p><p>In the past, Topman has collaborated with other well known companies such as Hypebeast and Vouchercloud.
Topman sales through the distribution channel range, including their own stores, either individually or with Topshop. Topman also uses franchising stores in department stores, such as Debenhams and Selfridges &amp; Co. Topman, which can also be passed through the international website on the Internet. Some of the products in the stock range of this website are exclusive websites. The Arcadia group has expanded to other parts of the world and is now available in many international countries, including the United States, Australia and the whole of Europe.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Topshop_and_Topman_(17th_December_2012).JPG" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ef/Topshop_and_Topman_%2817th_December_2012%29.JPG/220px-Topshop_and_Topman_%2817th_December_2012%29.JPG" decoding="async" width="220" height="124" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ef/Topshop_and_Topman_%2817th_December_2012%29.JPG/330px-Topshop_and_Topman_%2817th_December_2012%29.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ef/Topshop_and_Topman_%2817th_December_2012%29.JPG/440px-Topshop_and_Topman_%2817th_December_2012%29.JPG 2x" data-file-width="3872" data-file-height="2176" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Topshop_and_Topman_(17th_December_2012).JPG" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>A flagship Topshop and Topman in <a href="/wiki/Leeds" title="Leeds">Leeds</a> seen shortly after opening in December 2012.</div></div></div>
<p>Topman's main product offerings are fashionable men's clothing, covering everything from men's formal suits to informal indie-rock-influenced casual-styling ranges, along with footwear and fashion accessories. Topman also sells a specially-designed range of clothing at their flagship London store in an area nicknamed 'Lens'.
</p><p>Topman has collaborated with designers such as <a href="/wiki/Kim_Jones_(designer)" title="Kim Jones (designer)">Kim Jones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Markus_Lupfer" title="Markus Lupfer">Markus Lupfer</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Jensen_(fashion_designer)" title="Peter Jensen (fashion designer)">Peter Jensen</a>, as well as launching its own premium label ‘Topman Design’. After showing seasonally to the menswear press arena, Topman joined forces with Fashion East to produce 'MAN', a menswear event that takes place on schedule at the <a href="/wiki/London_Fashion_Week" title="London Fashion Week">London Fashion Week</a>, showcasing new menswear design as well as the Topman Design collection.
</p><p>In 2008, Topman collaborated with new label <a href="/wiki/The_Look_Presents" title="The Look Presents">The Look Presents</a>, the creation of rock-fashion author <a href="/wiki/Paul_Gorman" title="Paul Gorman">Paul Gorman</a> and Soho boutique owner, Max Karie, to launch 'the Look Presents'.<sup id="cite_ref-retailappointment.co.uk_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-retailappointment.co.uk-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This comprised four collections:
</p>
<ul><li>A range of T-shirts by the 70s label Wonder Workshop which was launched in June 2008.</li>
<li>A T-shirt collection by <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Waymouth" title="Nigel Waymouth">Nigel Waymouth</a>, founder of the King's Road store <a href="/wiki/Granny_Takes_a_Trip" title="Granny Takes a Trip">Granny Takes a Trip</a>, which was launched in August 2008.</li>
<li>Priceless, the ready-to-wear <a href="/wiki/Capsule_collection" class="mw-redirect" title="Capsule collection">capsule collection</a> by <a href="/wiki/Antony_Price" title="Antony Price">Antony Price</a>, designer for <a href="/wiki/Roxy_Music" title="Roxy Music">Roxy Music</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bryan_Ferry" title="Bryan Ferry">Bryan Ferry</a> and <a href="/wiki/Duran_Duran" title="Duran Duran">Duran Duran</a> which was launched in October 2008.</li>
<li>Stella McCartney for Topman, the accessory and ready-to-wear collection was launched in November 2009.</li></ul>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Topmanbath.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/24/Topmanbath.jpg/220px-Topmanbath.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/24/Topmanbath.jpg/330px-Topmanbath.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/24/Topmanbath.jpg/440px-Topmanbath.jpg 2x" data-file-width="720" data-file-height="540" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Topmanbath.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Topman <a href="/wiki/SouthGate,_Bath" title="SouthGate, Bath">SouthGate, Bath</a></div></div></div>
<p>Topman was nominated for the ‘Menswear Design of the Year’ award by the British Fashion Council, winner of ‘Best Fashion Retailer’ at the Maxim Style Awards for 2005 and winner of ‘Best High Street Retailer’ at the 2007 FHM Fashion and Grooming awards.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2011, Topman worked closely with <a href="/wiki/Teenage_Cancer_Trust" title="Teenage Cancer Trust">Teenage Cancer Trust</a>. The company worked in collaboration with children from the charity to produce a range of sunglasses designed by them whilst supporting the 'Rock your Shades' campaign in May 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-retailappointment.co.uk_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-retailappointment.co.uk-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In late 2011, Topman also debuted its line of male grooming products, featuring two men's fragrances, hair and body wash, anti-perspirant, <a href="/wiki/Hair_products" class="mw-redirect" title="Hair products">hair putty</a> and body spray.
</p><p>Towards the end of 2011, Topman began to release issues of its online magazine Topman Generation centering art, music and fashion. Its collections are often influenced by film or music, <a href="/wiki/Northern_soul" title="Northern soul">Northern soul</a> being one of the themes presented at <a href="/wiki/London_Fashion_Week" title="London Fashion Week">London Fashion Week</a> Men.
</p>
