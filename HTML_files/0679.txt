<p><b>Shelley Community Football Club</b> is an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Shelley,_West_Yorkshire" title="Shelley, West Yorkshire">Shelley</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Yorkshire" title="West Yorkshire">West Yorkshire</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> They are currently members of the <a href="/wiki/North_West_Counties_Football_League" title="North West Counties Football League">North West Counties League</a> Division One North and play at Storthes Hall Park.
</p>
