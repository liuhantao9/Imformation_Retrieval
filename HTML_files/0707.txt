	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Initiative for the Integration of the Regional Infrastructure of South America</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Initiative for the Integration of the Regional Infrastructure of South America</b> (<b>IIRSA</b>) is a development plan to link <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a>'s economies through new <a href="/wiki/Transportation" class="mw-redirect" title="Transportation">transportation</a>, <a href="/wiki/Energy" title="Energy">energy</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Telecommunications" class="mw-redirect" title="Telecommunications">telecommunications</a> projects.
</p><p>IIRSA investments are expected to integrate highway networks, river ways, hydroelectric dams and telecommunications links throughout the continent&#8212;particularly in remote, isolated regions&#8212;to allow greater trade and create a South American community of nations.
</p><p>The initiative was launched in late 2000 with the participation of the 12 countries of South America which form the <a href="/wiki/Union_of_South_American_Nations" title="Union of South American Nations">Union of South American Nations</a>. It is being supported by the <a href="/wiki/Corporaci%C3%B3n_Andina_de_Fomento" class="mw-redirect" title="Corporación Andina de Fomento">Corporación Andina de Fomento</a> (CAF), the <a href="/wiki/Inter-American_Development_Bank" title="Inter-American Development Bank">Inter-American Development Bank</a> (IDB) and the <a href="/wiki/Plata_Basin_Financial_Development_Fund" title="Plata Basin Financial Development Fund">River Plate Basin Financial Development Fund</a> (<a href="/wiki/FONPLATA" class="mw-redirect" title="FONPLATA">FONPLATA</a>). Together the three institutions form the Technical Coordination Committee (CCT) which provides technical and financial support for IIRSA activities.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Region">Region</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Region">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The project is subdivided in several regions, called hubs.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Amazon_Hub">Amazon Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Amazon Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Andean_Hub">Andean Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Andean Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Southern_Andean_Hub">Southern Andean Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Southern Andean Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Capricorn_Hub">Capricorn Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Capricorn Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Central_Interoceanic_Hub">Central Interoceanic Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Central Interoceanic Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Guianese_Shield_Hub">Guianese Shield Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Guianese Shield Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Within the Guianese Shield Hub, 4 groups of projects have been identified.<sup id="cite_ref-presentation_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-presentation-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Group_1:_Interconnection_Venezuela-Brazil">Group 1: Interconnection Venezuela-Brazil</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Group 1: Interconnection Venezuela-Brazil">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The anchor project within the first group is the rehabilitation of the <a href="/wiki/Caracas" title="Caracas">Caracas</a>-<a href="/wiki/Manaus" title="Manaus">Manaus</a> road.<sup id="cite_ref-presentation_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-presentation-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Group_2:_Interconnection_Guyana-Brazil">Group 2: Interconnection Guyana-Brazil</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Group 2: Interconnection Guyana-Brazil">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The anchor project within the second group is the improvement of <a href="/wiki/Boa_Vista,_Roraima" title="Boa Vista, Roraima">Boa Vista</a>-<a href="/wiki/Bonfim,_Roraima" title="Bonfim, Roraima">Bonfim</a>-<a href="/wiki/Lethem,_Guyana" title="Lethem, Guyana">Lethem</a>-<a href="/wiki/Georgetown,_Guyana" title="Georgetown, Guyana">Georgetown</a> road.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The construction of the <a href="/wiki/Takutu_River_Bridge" title="Takutu River Bridge">Takutu River Bridge</a> between Guyana and Brazil on this road was a separate project within IIRSA,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> as is the construction of a deep water port in northern Guyana and a hydropower plant in Amaila, among others.<sup id="cite_ref-presentation_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-presentation-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Group_3:_Interconnection_Venezuela-Guyana-Suriname">Group 3: Interconnection Venezuela-Guyana-Suriname</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Group 3: Interconnection Venezuela-Guyana-Suriname">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The anchor project within the third group is the improvement and construction of the <a href="/wiki/Ciudad_Guayana" title="Ciudad Guayana">Ciudad Guayana</a>-<a href="/w/index.php?title=San_Mart%C3%ADn_de_Turumbang&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="San Martín de Turumbang (page does not exist)">San Martín de Turumbang</a>-<a href="/wiki/Linden,_Guyana" title="Linden, Guyana">Linden</a>-<a href="/wiki/Apoera" title="Apoera">Apoera</a>-<a href="/wiki/Paramaribo" title="Paramaribo">Paramaribo</a> road with a connection from Linden to Georgetown. Only the section between San Martín de Turumbang and Linden seems likely to be reconstructed in the imminent future.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The various bridges proposed on this route, including the international crossings on the <a href="/wiki/Cuyuni_River" title="Cuyuni River">Cuyuni River</a> (Venezuela-Guyana) and <a href="/wiki/Corentyne_River" class="mw-redirect" title="Corentyne River">Corentyne River</a> (Guyana-Suriname) are also projects within group 3. The section within Suriname of this route is part of the Southern <a href="/wiki/East-West_Link_(Suriname)" title="East-West Link (Suriname)">East-West Link</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Group_4:_Interconnection_Guyana-Suriname-French_Guiana-Brazil">Group 4: Interconnection Guyana-Suriname-French Guiana-Brazil</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Group 4: Interconnection Guyana-Suriname-French Guiana-Brazil">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The anchor project in the fourth group is the improvement of the Georgetown-<a href="/wiki/Albina,_Suriname" title="Albina, Suriname">Albina</a> road, which includes a bridge linking Albina with <a href="/wiki/Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni" title="Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni">Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni</a> in <a href="/wiki/French_Guiana" title="French Guiana">French Guiana</a> over the <a href="/wiki/Marowijne_River" class="mw-redirect" title="Marowijne River">Marowijne River</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The section within Suriname of this route is part of the Northern <a href="/wiki/East-West_Link_(Suriname)" title="East-West Link (Suriname)">East-West Link</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Oyapock_River_Bridge" title="Oyapock River Bridge">bridge between French Guiana and Brazil</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Oyapock_River" class="mw-redirect" title="Oyapock River">Oyapock River</a> and the improvement of the road between <a href="/wiki/Oiapoque" title="Oiapoque">Oiapoque</a> and <a href="/wiki/Macap%C3%A1" title="Macapá">Macapá</a> are also part of this group. It has been suggested that French Guiana, which is not part of the IIRSA project, be included in this respect as an observing partner.<sup id="cite_ref-presentation_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-presentation-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="MERCOSUR-Chile_Hub">MERCOSUR-Chile Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: MERCOSUR-Chile Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Paraguay-Parana_Waterway_Hub">Paraguay-Parana Waterway Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Paraguay-Parana Waterway Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Peru-Brazil-Bolivia_Hub">Peru-Brazil-Bolivia Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Peru-Brazil-Bolivia Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Southern_Hub">Southern Hub</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Southern Hub">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Criticism">Criticism</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Initiative_for_the_Integration_of_the_Regional_Infrastructure_of_South_America&amp;action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Criticism">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to <a href="/wiki/Conservation_International" title="Conservation International">Conservation International</a> scientist Tim Killeen, who conducted a study on the IIRSA,<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> the current plans could lead to the destruction of the <a href="/wiki/Amazon_rainforest" title="Amazon rainforest">Amazon rainforest</a> and have profound and far-reaching consequences.
</p><p>The study shows that cutting and burning of the forests could seriously imperil the multibillion-dollar agriculture industry of the <a href="/wiki/Rio_Plata" class="mw-redirect" title="Rio Plata">Rio Plata</a> basin, as well as destroy the ecosystems that are home to indigenous people. According to the study, the IIRSA would also wipe out some of Earth's richest storehouses of terrestrial and freshwater life and would negatively affect <a href="/wiki/Climate_change" title="Climate change">climate change</a> by releasing into the atmosphere the huge quantities of <a href="/wiki/Carbon_dioxide" title="Carbon dioxide">carbon dioxide</a> stored in the biomass of the tropical forest&#8212;estimated at about twenty times the world's total annual greenhouse gas emissions.
</p><p>According to Killeen, the IIRSA does not have to be destructive: "A visionary initiative such as IIRSA should be visionary in all of its dimensions, and should incorporate measures to ensure that the region’s renewable natural resources are conserved and its traditional communities strengthened."<sup id="cite_ref-enn_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-enn-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
